Gabrielle Chanel pop-up on Bond Street in London

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, April 2, 2024

Join Bazaar beauty director Katy Young for a reader event at the new Gabrielle Chanel pop-up on London's Old Bond Street.

During the Harper's Bazaar session, readers will enjoy an exclusive olfactive workshop to discover the secrets of Gabrielle Chanel with a fragrance expert, followed by a Q&A session with Katy Young where you will be able to ask her any of your burning beauty questions.

Attendees will receive a goodie bag and a copy of Harper's Bazaar, and refreshments will be served. The session with Katy Young will take place on Thursday 14 September from 4-5pm and then again from 5-6pm.

The Espace Gabrielle Chanel offers an immersive experience into the universe and personality of Gabrielle Chanel herself on Old Bond Street in the heart of London's Mayfair. The temporary space will conjure the world of Gabrielle Chanel played out through a journey of discovery, starting with a room peppered with Mademoiselle Chanel's favourite quotes and scented niches to experience the main ingredients of the fragrance. Follow the kaleidoscopic walls lined with faceted mirrors and discover films showcasing the inspiration behind the fragrance and the new film starring Kristen Stewart.
