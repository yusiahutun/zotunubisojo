Matt Mierswa (Jeopardy!) Biography, Age, Height, Wife, Wiki

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Matt Mierswa is an American personality and entered the show Jeopardy [38th season] on 19th July 2022. With the marking of his new beginning on the show, he has played well and turned out to be the second-day gamer on the stage. In his first episode, he won the amount of $29,201 and would be facing his competitors on Thursday, 21st July 2022. On the day, he would be standing against the two new contestants named Emmie Trammell and Stan Scoggins.

Early Life

As per his Facebook profile, Matt Mierswa lived in Manaus, Brazil and he was born in 1988. His full name is Matthew Mierswa currently resides in River Edge, New Jersey, and also spent some time in Glen Rock as well. Matt received his education from Villanova University with a degree of BA in Political Science and Government. Later from 2011 to 2014, he received his doctorate in law from Seton Hall University School of Law.

Matt Mierswa Career

Immediately after completing his education in 2014, he began his professional life and landed a job under Honorable Scott J. Moynihan, J.S.C. as Judicial Law Clerk. He stretched his work from August 2014 to August 2015. From September 2015 to January 2019, he worked as an assistant district attorney at New York County District Attorney’s Office. He worked as a litigation associate at Bressler, Amery & Ross, P.C. for two years and seven months. Since July 2021, he has been working as Corporate Counsel at Horizon Blue Cross Blue Shield of New Jersey in a full-time role.

Jeopardy Journey

On the 19th of July, Matt became a one-day winner by giving the correct answer to the final question of the episode. He defeated William Chou, who wagered all of his amounts in the final round and lost. On 20th July, Mierswa and another competitor Alison Tremble had a good start as both the competitors gave 16 correct answers. Mierswa had the total earnings [till then] as $6000 while Alison was $200 ahead of him. The third competitor Alfred Guy struggled hard with the clue and gave only seven correct answers with five incorrect ones, left with only -$200.

In the final round of Jeopardy on the day of 20th July 2022, both Matt and Alfred gave the right answers to the final question of the episode. Alison gave the wrong answer and lost the wagering amount. While Alfred had total earnings of $10,999 and Matt Mierswa had total earnings of $26,644.

Now, the coming up episode will mark the new beginning with the returning champion Matt Mierswa, who played brilliantly over the past two days. He would be now facing two competitors- operations specialist Emmie Trammell and senior vice president of marketing assets, Stan Scoggins.
