Lisa Snowdon's purse-friendly floral Topshop dress is selling out rapidly and we love it

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 22, 2024

The model has total flower power in printed frock

    George Clooney's ex-girlfriend Lisa Snowdon wowed viewers on This Morning as she presented the fashion segment of the show and showed off her style credentials in a wonderful floral number by high street store Topshop. The flower printed design features bold tones including orange, pink and green on a  black base and nailed three trends in one – the flower print, bell-sleeves and a glamorous pussy-bow neck tie. The high street item was cut in a flowing design which finished at the ankles in a midi-length. The great news about this nifty number is that it retails at an affordable £49 and is currently available online in all sizes – great news for fans of Lisa's cheerful look. She added another trending item – a pair of white stiletto ankle boots by Dune London. Lisa uploaded a mirror selfie to her Instagram account featuring her latest 'outfit of the day' and captioned the shot: "Spring has sprung and I for one couldn't be happier!"

    Lisa wore a blooming lovely dress on This Morning 

     Lisa is a huge fan of the high street and often showcases her fashion bargains on her social media platforms. In February she sent fans wild when she wore a bright red printed top from Zara, which set her back just £15.99!

    Lisa's dress is £49 from Topshop

    Shooting her campaign images for QVC – the company she has a jewellery line with - the 45-year-old model looked beautiful in the behind-the-scenes snap – and yet again, the bargain top came with a detachable pussy bow. Lisa opted not to wear the bow tied round her neck – instead choosing to accessorise with a selection of dainty gold necklaces from her range.  

    MORE: Lisa Snowdon dazzles in ZARA rainbow dress

    Lisa has an incredible figure admired by many. The model has spoken about her favourite part of her body – her endless legs: "I'm lucky I'm tall so therefore my legs are long". He least favourite, meanwhile is her arms and tummy as "they're a bit out of shape!"
