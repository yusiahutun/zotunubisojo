Lindsay Hubbard, Carl Radke Wedding Plans Before Breakup: Summer House

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Who Was Invited to Lindsay Hubbard and Carl Radke's Wedding?

Lindsay Hubbard and Carl Radke didn’t confirm their wedding guests before their breakup. However, it was expected that most of of the Summer House Season 8 cast would be invited. The main cast members for Summer House Season 8 included returning cast members Kyle Cooke, Amanda Batule, Paige DeSorbo, Ciara Miller, Danielle Olivera, and Gabby Prescod, as well as new cast members Jesse Solomon and West Wilson. The cast also included part-time cast members Sam Feher, Mya Allen, and Chris Leoni, as well as guests Kory Keefer and Andrea Denver.

In an interview on Watch What Happens Live in April 2023, Lindsay revealed that she wasn’t sure at the time if she wanted to invite Danielle—her former best friend whom she had a falling out with over Danielle’s concerns about Lindsay and Carl’s fast relationship—to her wedding. “I guess we’ll find out after the reunion,” she said. Lindsay also told host Andy Cohen that she was “hopeful” for a resolution between her and Danielle.

“As far as public[ly] mending our friendship, I think the challenge is that we haven’t really seen each other,” Lindsay said. “From what I’ve heard, she is working through some things in her personal life, and we’re planning a wedding, so I think that just, right now, we’re just on different paths.”

Danielle reacted to Lindsay’s hope for their relationship in her own interview with The Daily Dish. “I mean, it’s awesome to hear her say that, of course. But actions speak louder than words, especially in a public forum,” she said.

Danielle continued, “I’m going through a lot in my life right now, and it would be great to have her,” Danielle said. “But I think I’m going to leave it in her court, because I think I’ve done enough on my end. I’ve said what I needed to say. Time will tell, but I haven’t heard from her, so that, I think, says a lot.”

Danielle also hinted on the Mention It All podcast that her issues with Lindsay stemmed from her comments about her ex-boyfriend, Robert Sieber. “Either you’re going to reach out or you’re not. It bothers me that we haven’t gotten to that point yet, of reaching out, especially [with] what’s going on in my personal life with Robert,” Danielle said. “I thought there was like a hope that maybe she’d be like, ‘Hey, I know we’re not in a good place right now; we’re not speaking, but I just want to let you know that I heard what was going on and I’m here for you.’ Something like that. But no, she’s planning a wedding, so…”

While it’s unclear if Lindsay and Danielle are friends again, the two have hinted at their reconciled relationship with photos of them filming Summer House Season 8. “Made it to Montauk,” Lindsay captioned an Instagram photo in August 2023 of her sitting next to Danielle.

Since Lindsay and Carl’s breakup, fans have flooded their online wedding guest book with comments about how Danielle was right about their relationship. “Hi Carl. Sucks you blew off your friendship with Danielle to marry that crazy Blonde,” wrote a user named “Matt.” “RIP yall. Danielle was right,” a user named “Karma Queen” wrote.
