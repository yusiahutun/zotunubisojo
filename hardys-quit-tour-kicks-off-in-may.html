Hardys Quit!! Tour Kicks Off In May

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 8, 2024

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess) — Big Loud Records recording artist Hardy announced plans for his 15-date summer Quit!! Tour.

Produced by Live Nation, the tour hits the ground at Hollywood Casino Amphitheatre in St. Louis on May 30 and concludes at Credit Union 1 Amphitheatre in Tinley Park, Ill. on July 27.

Kip Moore, Travis Denning, Ella Langley, and Stephen Wilson Jr. are scheduled to join Hardy as special guests for the tour on select dates.

The tour shares the same name with Hardy’s latest single, an autobiographical and referential spoken word track that recounts his rise on the Nashville music scene.

“Thank you for inspiring me to be great,” Hardy says. “I guess sometimes holding a grudge is a good thing.”

Tickets for the Quit!! tour go on sale Friday, Jan. 19 at 10 a.m. local time with presales starting on January 16th.
