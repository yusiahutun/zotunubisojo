What is the biggest K-pop fandom in the world? Top 10 list

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, April 17, 2024

South Korea's K-Pop is a global phenomenon. Most K-Pop groups have amassed a gigantic following worldwide. The fandoms have played a critical role in helping the K-Pop groups achieve international fame. There are several K-Pop fandoms, but some have stood out because of the population of their fans. So what is the biggest K-pop fandom in the world?

What is a K-pop fandom? K-pop fandom is a term used to refer to the fans of a K-pop group. Most fandoms are created immediately after a K-pop group makes its debut. During the launch of the fandom, the management of the K-pop group releases information about the fandom name and the meaning of the name. Each fandom has got its official color and lightstick.

There are so many K-pop groups out there, but some have stood out because they have amassed a gigantic following worldwide. But which K-pop group has the most fans? According to Seoul Space, the following fandoms have the highest number of fans.

1. BTS - ARMY

This is the most famous fandom in the world. ARMY is renowned for its status as BTS's fandom. The fandom name is an acronym for Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth. It made its debut in July 2013. ARMY boasts more than 40 million fans worldwide.

The impact of this fandom was felt in 2017 when BTS garnered more than 300 million votes to win the coveted Billboard's Top Social Artist award.

2. BLACKPINK- Blinks

Blinks is the second biggest fandom in the world. It is renowned for its status as the official fandom for BLACKPINK. The fandom name is derived from the K-pop group's name, i.e., the first two letters and the last three letters. Blinks was founded on January 14, 2017.

Since the fandom was founded, the fan base has increased steadily. Currently, the membership of the fandom is estimated at more than 38 million people. Thanks to its loyal fan base, BLACKPINK has risen through the ranks to become one of the most popular K-pop groups in the world.

3. EXO - EXO-L

This is one of the top K-pop fandoms. It is the official fandom for EXO. The L included in the fandom name stands for love. Fans of the K-pop group are called Stars/Aeris. EXO-L was established on August 5, 2014. It is one of the most popular K-pop fandoms, with a fan base of more than 10 million followers.

4. Red Velvet - ReVeluv

ReVeluv is one of the most famous K-pop girl groups fandoms. It is the official fandom name for the famous K-pop girl group Red Velvet. ReVe is derived from the first two letters of the group name. Luv stands for the love that exists between the K-pop group and the fandom.

The members of the K-pop group refer to their fans using the term luvies. ReVeluv was established on April 26, 2017, three years after Red Velvet made its debut. Its membership has increased steadily. The group has amassed a gigantic following on all the social media platforms. It has more than 12 million followers on Instagram.

5. Twice - ONCE

ONCE is the official fandom name for Twice. Twice is a famous K-pop girl group that debuted under JYP Entertainment in October 2015. The fandom was founded on November 4, 2015.

Most fans love the fandom name since it pairs perfectly with the K-pop group name, ONCE then Twice. ONCE boasts tons of loyal fans worldwide. The dedicated fans are the reason why Twice has risen through the ranks to become a dominant K-pop girl group.

6. Super Junior - ELF

ELF is the official fandom name for the Super Junior K-pop group. Super Junior, famously known as SuJu, is a famous K-pop boy group that debuted under SM Entertainment in 2005. The fandom name is an acronym that stands for Ever Lasting Friends.

This fandom name was chosen by Leeteuk. The fans are the friends of the K-pop group, always supporting them through thick and thin. They are the reason why Super Junior is where it is today. It has tons of loyal fans from across the globe.

7. BIGBANG - V.I.P

V.I.P. is the official fandom name for BIGBANG. The fandom name was derived from the K-pop group's song titled V.I.P. This song was included in the second single album. V.I.P. is an acronym that stands for Very Important Person.

BIGBANG chose this fandom name because it considers fans very important. Thanks to V.I.P's support, BIGBANG has cemented its position as one of the best K-pop boy groups of all time. The fandom boasts of legions of fans worldwide.

8. TXT – MOA

The list of powerful K-pop fandoms cannot be complete without the mention of MOA. MOA is the official fandom name for TXT. TXT stands for Tomorrow by Together. The K-pop boy group was established in March 2019 under Big Hit Entertainment.

TXT announced the fandom name on August 22, 2019. TXT boasts millions of fans worldwide who have helped it to become one of the leading K-pop groups of the next generation.

9. StrayKids - Stays

Stays is the official fandom name for StrayKids. StrayKids is a famous K-pop boy band that debuted in 2017 under JYP Entertainment. Stays was founded in August 2018. The slogan for the fandom is "You make Stray Kids STAY."

Straykids chose this fandom name because they consider fans to be the reason why and where they can stay. Thanks to the support from Stays, StrayKids has risen through the ranks to become one of the most popular K-pop groups.

10. SHINee – SHINee World

SHINee World is the official fandom name for SHINee. SHINee is a South Korean boy band that debuted in 2008 under SM Entertainment. The fans of SHINee are referred to as Shawols. This name was created by combining the words Shinee and World.

They chose this name because they consider fans to be very important. SHINee World is one of the fandoms with the highest number of dedicated fans who have helped SHINee to be where it is today.
