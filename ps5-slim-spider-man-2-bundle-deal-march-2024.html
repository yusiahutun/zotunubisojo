PS5 Slim Spider-Man 2 Bundle Drops to Its Lowest Price Yet

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Countless PS5 deals and discounts were available during Black Friday and the holidays. If you missed out, now you can get the newer PS5 Slim Spider-Man 2 bundle for the lowest price we've seen yet, only $449.

If you've been debating whether you should upgrade to the PS5 Slim, this deal might make you think twice. Best Buy is now offering the PS5 Slim with a digital copy of Spider-Man 2 for only $449 ($50 off), which is a great price as the new bundle's first discount.

We saw the regular PlayStation 5 drop to $499 late last year, then again a few weeks ago, but this deal drops the price even further while getting you the latest PS5 console at the same time. And for anyone who's seen the original PS5, it's enormous, so this is a welcome change.

While the new "Slim" model still has the same general design and shape, it's about 30% smaller and quite a bit lighter than the previous disc and discless versions. It doesn't come with a vertical stand in the box anymore, and if you still want one, it'll be another $30. The Slim comes with two front-facing USB-C ports, 1TB of storage, and more. Either way, you're getting the latest and greatest alongside a much-anticipated game, all for only $449.

PS5 Slim Spider-Man 2 Bundle

The new PS5 Slim is compact, lightweight, and packs front-facing USB-C ports with 1TB of storage. Grab it today with the Spider-Man 2 bundle. 

Buy it Now

Best Buy's PS5 Slim with Spider-Man 2 bundle is available in stores and online. More importantly, the deal is also arriving at the perfect time, with Final Fantasy VII Rebirth hitting shelves. Just like the original PS5 model, you also get backwards compatibility with most PS4 games, so you can play everything from Journey to The Last of Us Part II to Red Dead Redemption 2 in one box.

Once you buy one, feel free to come back and check out our guide on how to transfer screenshots or videos from your PS5 and why you'd even want to. It's not clear how long the deal will last.
