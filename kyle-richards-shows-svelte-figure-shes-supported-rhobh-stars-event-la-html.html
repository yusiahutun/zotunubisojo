Kyle Richards shows off her svelte figure as she's supported byRHOBH co-stars at event in LA

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, October 3, 2024

Kyle Richards put her slim frame on display while hosting a Night of Music event in Los Angeles on Tuesday.

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star, 54, showed off her toned arms in a black tank top that was tucked into a pair of tight vinyl pants. 

The Scream actress - who recently explained how she achieved her trim figure after denying that she used the help of Ozempic - wore her brunette tresses halfway pinned up, and accessorized with a silver necklace that read 'Life is beautiful.'  

She was joined by a number of her former and current RHOBH co-stars, including Teddi Mellencamp, Dorit Kemsley, Garcelle Beauvais, Camille Grammer, and Sutton Stracke. 

Also supporting Richards were her family members, including sister Kim Richards, and her four daughters: Alexia, Portia, and Sophia Umansky, as well as her eldest Farrah Aldjufrie.

It comes after Kyle previously denied claims that she used the controversial weight loss drug Ozempic to achieve her skinny figure. 

The drug is usually prescribed to individuals who have type 2 diabetes and need to improve their blood sugar.

In 2021, the FDA (Food and Drug Administration) approved Ozempic to be used by individuals suffering from general obesity.

Richards spoke out against the 'frustrating' rumors last month in February, and told Page Six at the time, 'I had never heard of it.'

'I had heard of it when they accused me, but I’d already lost weight by the time I had heard about Ozempic, so it was really frustrating to me,' she explained to the outlet.

The star continued, 'What I don’t want to get lost in this is that I want to be able to inspire people.'

Kyle expressed that her slimmer figure was simply due to a healthier lifestyle as well as sobriety.

'I stopped drinking almost seven months ago, I don’t eat bad, sugar, pizza, donuts, carbs … so I’d rather be inspiring than people making up stories about me,' and added that she stops by the gym, 'for two hours every day.'
