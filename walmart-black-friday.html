When is Walmart’s Black Friday sale?

Instead of hosting just one Walmart Black Friday sale this year, the retailer has split it up into two sales collectively coined the “Walmart Black Friday Deals” events. Two Black Friday sales and one Cyber Monday sale is ample opportunity to save.

  • Event 1 (begins online on Wednesday, November 8 at 3 p.m. ET and continues in stores on Friday, November 10):
    • Walmart+ early access runs from 12 p.m. ET to 3 p.m. ET on Wednesday, November 8
  • Event 2 (begins online on Wednesday, November 22 at 3 p.m. ET and continues in stores on Friday, November 24): 
    • Walmart+ early access runs from 12 p.m. ET to 3 p.m. ET on Wednesday, November 22

Who can shop Walmart’s Black Friday sale?

While other major sale events require a membership to shop the discounts, Walmart’s Black Friday Deals event will be fully open to the public throughout the two-part sale. That said, those with a Walmart+ membership will get three hours of early access to all of Walmart’s Black Friday and Cyber Monday sale events.

“Exclusive first access to the most coveted holiday deals coupled with free shipping, gives Walmart+ members a shopping superpower for the most highly-anticipated event of the year,” said Venessa Yates, senior vice president and general manager of Walmart+. “The fact that customers can now unlock this benefit – not to mention everything else that comes with membership – for only $49 right now, makes Walmart+ the gift you’ll be grateful for well beyond the holiday season.”

What will be discounted during Walmart’s Black Friday sale?

What exactly can you get for less during Walmart’s Black Friday sale? Only the biggest brand names, like Apple, Dyson, LEGO, Ninja, Xbox, and Samsung, among others. You’ll find steep markdowns across all categories, including apparel, electronics, home, toys, and more.

If you’re trying to cross off some big-ticket items from your list, such as a smart TV, robot vacuum, or laptop, Walmart is your one-stop shop. Don’t let the little things slip through the cracks, though; the Walmart Black Friday Deals event has notable discounts on stocking stuffers and toiletries, too.

“Our customers are going to be wowed by the amazing savings on popular gifts, many of which can only be found at Walmart. We have very exciting Black Friday events planned, with the type of extraordinary value, incredible assortment, and easy, convenient shopping experience only Walmart can offer,” Latriece Watkins, executive vice president and chief merchandising officer for Walmart U.S., said in a press release. “We’ve worked to make sure it’ll be our customers’ best Black Friday experience yet.”

Can I shop for refurbished items during Walmart’s Black Friday sale?

Yes—in fact, you’re going to want to take advantage of any refurbished products (think Dyson hot tools for hundreds of dollars off) to save big during Walmart’s Black Friday sale. The retailer usually offers higher discounts on these items, but rest assured, they’re still in good condition.

FAQS

Do I need to be a Walmart+ member to shop Walmart Black Friday deals?

While you don’t need to be a Walmart+ member to shop the sale, there are plenty of perks to signing up for a membership. When you’re a Walmart+ member, you’ll receive free delivery from your local Walmart store on items like fresh groceries, free shipping with no order minimum on all purchases, a free Paramount+ membership alongside your Walmart+ membership, and free pick-up return orders (you won’t have to repackage or send back your items—Walmart will come to you!)

How do I sign up to be a Walmart+ member?

To celebrate the Walmart Black Friday Deals event, customers can get one year of unlimited free delivery, incredible fuel savings, free shipping, a Paramount+ subscription, and more for just $49 ($4.08 per month) by signing up for Walmart+ by November 8. A membership typically costs $98 per year ($8.17 per month), so hop on this half-off, limited-time deal fast.
