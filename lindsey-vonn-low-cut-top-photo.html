Lindsey Vonn Takes the Plunge In One of the Lowest Cut Bodysuits Ever!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, April 22, 2024

Hello there, Lindsey Vonn!

The Olympic gold medalist stepped out to Craig's in Los Angeles on Monday night showing some serious skin, flashing killer cleavage in a low-cut House of CB bodysuit that nearly went down to her belly button. She paired the risque number with skin-tight black pants, nude heels, a leather choker and a smoky eye.

The 31-year-old blonde beauty looked amazing from all angles as she was photographed heading to her car after dinner -- and was clearly comfortable with her curves. But, earlier in the day, Vonn admitted that wasn't always the case.

Tiger Woods' ex appeared on "The Talk" Monday morning, where the topic turned to Chloe Grace Moretz's recent interview about wanting to get plastic surgery when she was younger. The now 18-year-old added that she was glad she didn't, something Vonn chimed in on.

"I was taller than everyone my age," she said of her own teen year. "I have no boobs, I have a big butt. Now it's popular to have a big butt, but before it was not."

"I'm glad I didn't change anything, I wanted a boob job, but I'm glad I didn't [get one]," she continued. "You learn so much about myself, every year you get older and more mature and you realize actually, that's pretty hot. I'm psyched with my body, I love it."

And we can see why!

Vonn is currently recovering from a string of injuries, the most recent being a knee bust up she sustained during the Andorra super G race in February.
