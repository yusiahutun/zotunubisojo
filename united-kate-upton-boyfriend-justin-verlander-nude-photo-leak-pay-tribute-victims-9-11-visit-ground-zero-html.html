Kate Upton and boyfriend Justin Verlander put nude photo leak behind them

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

It has been a tough time for Kate Upton and baseball star Justin Verlander, but the pair decided to reflect on other's sacrifices instead of their own sorrows on Tuesday.

The couple were both targeted in the nude celebrity hacking incident, known as the fappening, with their private photographs of each other released to the world.

However, on Tuesday, the couple were putting the photo leak behind them, as they paid tribute to the victims of 9/11 in New York.

Paying tribute: Kate Upton and boyfriend Justin Verlander visited Ground Zero in New York on Tuesday

Kate, 22, and her 31-year-old pitcher beau stopped by Ground Zero, the location where the Twin Towers previously stood prior to the September 11, 2001, attacks.

Looking solemn, the pair walked around the site through a still in construction section.

Kate and Justin only recently arrived in New York after the 31-year-old and his Detroit Tigers played and lost the series final to Baltimore Orioles on Sunday.

The Sports Illustrated star was there to support her boyfriend and had even helped to encourage fans to wear all blue per the pitcher's request to help the team get over the line. 

Tragic memorial: Looking solemn, the pair walked around the site through a still in construction section where the Twin Towers once stood prior to the terror attacks

The Other Woman star also took to twitter ahead of the game to rev up fan support. 

The couple, like the Tigers, have not always been winners when it comes to the game of love.
