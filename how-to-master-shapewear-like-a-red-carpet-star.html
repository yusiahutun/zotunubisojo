What shapewear to wear with what

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

It used to be that women used to keep their shapewear to themselves, but times are changing. As celebrities open up about the control garments they wear to help maintain smooth, red-carpet silhouettes, we've created a guide to what underpinnings to choose for different silhouettes.

Based on tricks of the trade from the stars and the expert advice of Ultimo's head of design Sharon Bell, this is how to master shapewear effectively, from the best colour combinations to the key styles for a slimming effect.

1. Do try little boxers

Zoe Kravitz told InStyle that she tried an alternative form of Spanx for the Golden Globes: "I'm just wearing underwear. Calvin Klein. Little boxers."

Getty Images

2. All-in-one shapes create the smoothest silhouettes

"An all-in-one shape, such as a smoothing body or shaping slip is ideal as there are no visible lines," said Sharon Bell. "An alternative would be a super-high-waist brief that sits just below the bra line to create a super-sleek silhouette."

3. Gwyneth Paltrow swears by Spanx

"There are these great things called Spanx, and they just squeeze you in. It's terrific! That's how all the Hollywood girls do it," says Paltrow. Sienna Miller, Emily Blunt and Cate Blanchett are all fans.

4. For a smooth stomach, only two shapewear pieces will do

"Look for lingerie with a hidden support panel built in to streamline the tummy," Bell told us. "Or maybe try a high-waist brief that has extra control to smooth that particular area."

5. Gaffer tape is Kim Kardashian's go-to for extra lift

She explained on her app that she's used "everything from duct tape to packing tape to masking tape".

6. Multi-way bras effectively hide bra straps

"Ultimo's has a 'wear five ways' styling that can be styled in various combinations such as halterneck or asymmetric," said Bell. "Many of our core collection shapes come with a racer-back hook so that the straps can be gathered at the back and hidden depending on the shape and style of your dress."

Ultimo\'s Compilation collection

7. Naked dresses are rarely naked dresses

Rihanna and Jennifer Lawrence are both fans of the bodysuit, which offers the illusion of appearing nude without showing anything too private.

8. A "booty bra" will create the illusion of a perky derriere

The underwear flattens the stomach, while expandable derriere fabric results in a Kim Kardashian-esque behind.

9. If in doubt about colour combinations, always go nude

"This really depends on the individual," said Bell. "Nude can be worn under any colour, and black under dark colours is always a staple, but there is much more variety in what shades of shapewear you can buy now, so there's more opportunity to match to your dress of choice."

Getty Images

10. The push-up bra is not dead

Jamie Chung, Kylie Jenner and Khloe Kardashian have all admitted that they need a little extra lift. "Try a 'wear your own bra' style," said Bell. "The Ultimo Signature slip or Contour body are the perfect solutions as you can team both pieces with your favourite cleavage-enhancing bra for maximum uplift and body contouring combined."

11. And remember, shapewear can be worn as outerwear

Look to Gigi Hadid wearing a Bordelle bustier in Zayn Malik's Pillowtalk, or to Ultimo's new catwalk collaboration with House of Holland – four black pieces that could either be worn to underpin a look or as a fashion statement in its own right.
