Manuel García-Rulfo Lapuente (born February 25, 1981) is a Mexican actor. He is best known for his starring role as lawyer Mickey Haller in the Lincoln Lawyer TV series, as well as in films such as Cake and Bless Me, Ultima, and the television program From Dusk till Dawn: The Series. He also starred as one of the title characters in the 2016 remake of The Magnificent Seven.
