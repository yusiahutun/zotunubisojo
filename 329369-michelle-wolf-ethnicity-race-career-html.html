Who is Michelle Wolf? Get to know her ethnicity, race, career, more

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Michelle Wolf is an American comedian, actress, writer, and television personality. Over the years, she has expanded her profile to become a household name in the US.

Before fame, Michelle Wolf was just an ordinary standup comedian. She got her big break when she appeared in the Late Night show alongside Seth Meyers in 2014. Wolf also became famous when she became the fifth woman to perform a monologue at the Correspondents Dinner held at the White House on 28th April 2018.
