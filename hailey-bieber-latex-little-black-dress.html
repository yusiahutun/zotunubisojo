Hailey Biebers Patent Leather Little Black Dress Has Our Heartbeats Soaring

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Hailey Bieber's latest look continues to prove that her date night style is unmatched.

Spotted in London, the Rhode founder cut an elegant figure by putting a sultry twist on the little black dress. She wore a body-hugging, one-shoulder minidress by LaQuan Smith made of shiny black patent leather. The rest of her ensemble coordinated with the classic color scheme, with the model opting for sheer black tights from Calzedonia, black pointed slingback heels with peep toes, and a black shoulder bag by Saint Laurent.

Though she forwent a necklace, she made a statement with her other jewelry, wearing a sparkling pair of circular gold-and-diamond earrings and a gold bracelet.

MEGA//Getty Images

Hailey wore another take on the LBD when she joined husband Justin Bieber at a Moncler event earlier this week.

Then, she looked casually cool in the ivory Moncler Genius x Alyx Fraxinus puffer vest, which she layered over a black long-sleeved top. For the bottom half of her look, she wore a black cargo miniskirt from WARDROBE.NYC and black strappy lace-up heels adorned with bright red rosettes from Magda Butrym. Accessories included black sunglasses, a black clutch from Bottega Veneta, and bulky silver earrings.

Meanwhile, the "What Do You Mean?" singer wore an electric blue puffer bomber jacket with a white T-shirt, baggy blue jeans, white sneakers, and orange-tinted oval sunglasses with a white frame.

MEGA//Getty Images

Hailey opened up about her and Justin's relationship in an interview for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story.

"He's still the person that I wanna be rushing back to," she said. "I might fly somewhere and go do a job, but I can't wait to come back and hang out. And I feel like that's because of the effort that's been put in on both sides."
