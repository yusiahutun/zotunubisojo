Rihanna Reveals Her Fashion Icks

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 2, 2024

As a bonafide style icon, Rihanna has worn it all — from that sunshine yellow dress at the 2015 Met Gala to her sheer crystal-studded Adam Selman gown that nearly broke the internet. But sometimes even fashion queens have their regrets, and Rih is no different.

This week, at the launch of her Fenty x Puma Creeper Phatty Earth Tone shoe in London, the singer broke down a few of her favorite fashion moments over the years while speaking with British Vogue, as well as which ones now give her the "ick." 

Counting the backless Stella McCartney gown she wore to the 2014 Met Gala afterparty and the white crop top and maxi skirt she wore to the main event among her best looks, Rihanna went on to reveal that any outfits that showed off her nipples are ones that she wouldn't wear again. 

"It's gonna sound hypocritical because I did so much shit in my life. I had my nipples out, I had my panties out," said the Fenty Beauty founder. "But now those are the things that as a mom, an evolved young lady — emphasis on young — things that I just feel like I would never do, I'm like, 'Oh my god, I really did that? Nips out?'"

Rather than rely on her own fashion prowess, when it comes to dressing her two sons — RZA, 23 months, and 7-month-old Riot, Rihanna says she looks to their dad and her boyfriend, A$AP Rocky, for inspiration.

"When I dress them, I always try to dress them like Rocky," she previously told Entertainment Tonight. "I always envision dressing a girl, right? We all do as women, like, 'Oh, I'm going to dress her in these cute little things,' but then you get sons and you're like, 'What do I do?' and I was like, 'You know what, I have the biggest hack — their dad.'"
