The 80s-Themed Totally Tubular Touring Festival Kicks Off In June

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 22, 2024

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Sound Talent Group’s 80s-themed Totally Tubular touring festival will make its debut in 17 markets this year with a lineup that includes Bow Wow Wow, Thompson Twins, and Thomas Dolby, among others.

“This is a dream lineup for those who love the music of the early 1980’s, and for those who want to relive the days when life was…plain and simply–a total party,” says Jon Pleeter, CPO (Chief Party Officer) of Totally Tubular Festival. “You wore dayglow, you wore parachute pants, you had big hair, perms and more perms, mullets, leg warmers, along with tons of buttons and lots of rubber bracelets. You wore sunglasses at night. The choruses were big, and the hooks were bigger—the party didn’t end.”

The touring festival kicks off on June 28th at the Santa Barbara Bowl In Santa Barbara, California and concludes at Riverbend Music Center Amphitheatre in Cincinnati on July 27th.

Other artists announced for the inaugural lineup include Men Without Hats, Tom Bailey, Modern English, The Romantics, The Plimsouls, and Tommy TuTone (select markets.)

A portion of proceeds from ticket sales will go towards food banks locally in each market.
