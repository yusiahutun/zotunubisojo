Rosario Dawson's Changing Looks

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 31, 2024

Read on to see Rosario Dawson's beauty transformation throughout the years.

1998

After making her debut in Kids, Dawson appeared in He Got Game and Side Streets.

1999

The multi-ethnic beauty hit the premiere of Light It Up sporting very '90s wine-hued lipstick.

2001

Dawson played with red highlights-and played bass in rock spoof Josie and the Pussycats.

2002

Loose bombshell waves for the Men in Black II premiere.

2003

She hit the premiere of The Rundown with sleek, shiny strands and peach-hued makeup.

2004

Dawson's role in Alexander alongside a bleached-blond Colin Farrell inspired her edgy platinum locks. "At one point the toner turned my eyebrows cement-white and my hair orange," the natural brunette joked.

2005

Back to long dark waves for the premiere of Sin City.

2006

The actress opted for a sleek bob for her cool-girl turn in Clerks II (and her visit to the Critics' Choice Awards).

2006

Chic silver shadow for the Costume Institute Gala.

2007

The Death Proof star framed her perfect features with blunt retro fringe at the Vanity Fair Oscars bash.

2008

Dawson got grown-up glam with a sleek chignon and smoky eye makeup at the Art of Elysium gala.

2008

The Seven Pounds lead chose loose, easy curls for the premiere.

2009

She walked the red carpet at the Global Green Millenium Awards with straight, highlighted locks.

2010

For the MTV Video Music Awards, the Unstoppable star drew focus to her full lips with a bold red hue.

2011

Dawson swept her waves to the side for the Screen Actors Guild Awards.

2012

A low ponytail, sheer gloss and cat-eye liner at the Vanity Fair Oscars party.

2013

For the Golden Globes, Dawson's makeup artist Mai Quynh used combination of Laura Mercier shadows to create a soft, smoky eye, starting with the shimmery copper Terracotta. "I applied it from the lash line to her crease, blending it softly," said Quynh. "Then I layered Laura Mercier's Eye Shadow in Coffee Ground, smudging the color along the lash line."

2014

Dawson opts for shoulder-length hair on the red carpet.

2015

The Daredevil star rocks bouncy waves on the red carpet.

2016

The actress paired carefree wave and pared down makeup with a bright smile at a Voto Latino event.

2017

Dawson showed off a dewy glow at an event in Las Vegas, pairing her radiant skin with an icy blue eye shadow and a bold but natural lip.
