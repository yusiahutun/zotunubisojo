They travel the world in private jets, live in opulent residences, and run multimillion-dollar humanitarian projects. Nevertheless, the criticism leveled at certain pastors for being immensely affluent and preaching prosperity sparks an unending argument about how wealthy preachers should or should not be. If you want to know who the richest pastor in the world is right now, look at the list of preachers' net worths below.

Today's pastors no longer sit back and wait for the congregation to fund their lifestyles. Most are educated and build other careers before venturing into pastoral work. Furthermore, religious leaders are commercial titans and intelligent investors. They preach the prosperity gospel and make it upon themselves to set an excellent example for their congregations. As a result, their followers pick up on financial concepts from them.
