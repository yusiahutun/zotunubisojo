8 Websites to Help You Keep Up With Fashion Trends

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

With fashion trends changing so rapidly, it can be tough to keep up with them. The internet contains a lot of fashion advice, particularly on social media, but not all of it is worth paying heed to.

Therefore, we’ve curated this list of the eight best websites to keep up with fashion trends. Whether you want to update your wardrobe, dress up for a special event, or just want to know what's in vogue, these websites can help you.

1. TRENDZOOM

A London-based company, TRENDZOOM creates forecast reports for fashion trends, covering both apparel and accessories. It forecasts fashion trends for both men and women.

These reports contain images and sketches, along with a description of trending fashion. For each season, it predicts fashion trends based on color, design, concept, and fashion.

TRENDZOOM has a beautiful gallery for street style fashion images. Besides the in-depth reports, it shares the fashion news and a frequent newsletter.

Access to TRENDZOOM reports requires a subscription, with four different plans available. The basic plan costs around $100 per month, while the company plan costs $5,000 per month. The site offers an academic plan, but you’ll have to request a quote for it.

However, if you’re hesitant to subscribe, you can sign up for a free account and access sample reports.

2. Elle

Elle is a popular print and digital magazine for women that covers an array of things but focuses mainly on fashion, beauty, and style.

On its website, you can read several articles, reports, and news about the latest fashion trends. These include reports on different fashion trends for each season, street style fashion, and celebrity styles.

Moreover, it offers articles on the latest makeup, hairstyles, and skincare trends. Besides fashion, you can also read about horoscopes, celebrities, and entertainment.

It has a newsletter that covers all these topics. However, if you need fashion news, you can follow the specific RSS feed. The one-year subscription to this magazine costs $10, but there’s a ton of free content available on its website.

3. Glamour

Part of the Condé Nast family, Glamour is a women's magazine that covers fashion, beauty, entertainment, wellness, and culture.

The fashion section of Glamour provides you with the latest fashion trends, inspirations for outfits, celebrity styles, and handpicked fashion stuff.

It has a dedicated section for plus-size fashion on its site. Among the four newsletters, one focuses on beauty and one on shopping.

Glamour has websites for different regions, so you can expect to get location-relevant fashion trends for women. Those who need to keep up with fashion trends and news should surely bookmark this site.

4. Vogue

Vogue isn't something new to anyone who has an interest in fashion. The renowned magazine has over 8 million monthly print readers in the US alone.

But in case you don’t know, the Vogue website too features numerous articles and reports on fashion trends. From street style photos to celebrity style, you can find a ton of inspirations for your outfits.

It covers the fashion shows, giving you insights into the emerging trends in the fashion industry. You can also watch videos or visit the image gallery.

To get fashion tips and trends directly to your inbox, you can subscribe to Vogue newsletters. Like Glamour, Vogue is a brand of Condé Nast and has several region-specific sites.

5. theFashionSpot

theFashionSpot is the leading website for fashion advice and trends. With a focus on diversity and body positivity, the website discusses a variety of fashion and beauty topics.

theFashionSpot covers fashion events and runway shows, sharing the best looks, outfits, and emerging trends. The celebrity section inspires you with celebrities' images and outfits. You can read about skincare, makeup, and self-care products.

Fashionistas can also discuss fashion trends and tips in theFashionSpot's active forum.

6. Who What Wear

Founded in 2006, Who What Wear shares fashion and style advice for women, aiming to make style more accessible.

Who What Wear has an entire section on its site dedicated to fashion trends. Here, you can find the trends for different seasons, shoes, handbags, denim, work-from-home outfits, etc. Other than trending fashion, you can learn about celebrity style, runways, and street style.

Moreover, Who What Wear shares outfit inspirations for occasions like weddings and New Year's Eve. The site also features shopping guides and beauty advice. Who What Wear has a UK-specific site as well, which covers nearly all the same topics.

7. The Impression

Covering both men and women, The Impression publishes in-depth fashion trend reports, features, and news. However, access to this website requires a subscription.

The Trends category on its website covers the latest and seasonal trends. These include men's and women's apparel and accessories trends, categorized by colors and styles.

Besides this, you can follow fashion news, keep up with the runway shows, and see the stylish ad campaigns of fashion brands. The Impression also covers street style fashion and publishes short fashion videos.

The Impression’s subscription costs $22 per month. There is no trial available, but only a 7-day money-back guarantee.

8. Harper’s Bazaar

Founded in 1867, Harper’s Bazaar is a reputed American magazine for women’s fashion. Besides its print and digital magazine, Harper’s Bazaar has a website that covers fashion trends, beauty, celebrity news, and culture.

Harper’s Bazaar’s website features a section for fashion trends and the latest products roundups. Here, you can learn about seasonal, runway, and occasion-wise trends. The Bazaar Bride section covers bridal fashion, beauty, and wedding planning tips.

There are features covering everything from skincare and makeup to fitness. Among the other topics on the site are travel, music, movies, TV, and relationships.

The first-year subscription to Harper’s Bazaar magazine costs $10. But the website is free to access, and so is its newsletter.

Stay on Top of Fashion Trends

From colors and design to fabrics and style, these websites spot and predict the emerging trends in fashion. Most of these cover beauty, runway shows, and street style photos which are interesting topics for fashion lovers.

So whether you’re shopping for your wedding, adding new items to your closet, or planning your vacations, these websites can help you look your best.

If you want to follow these trends on the go, you can install an app to stay on top of fashion trends.
