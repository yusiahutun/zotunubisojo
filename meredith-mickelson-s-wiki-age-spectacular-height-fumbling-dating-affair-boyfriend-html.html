Meredith Mickelson’s Wiki-like Bio:

Meredith Mickelson is a model and social media sensation. She was born on July 1, 1999, in Atlanta, GA.  

Meredith recently celebrated her birthday and shared the picture of birthday celebration on her official Instagram account.

Meredith is popular in different social networking sites like Twitter and Instagram with more than million of followers. In 2012, she created her Twitter account and a YouTube channel called Meg & Mer with her friend Megan Donoho.

By the age of eighteen, Meredith has gained popularity working with Next Model Management Miami, Freedom Los Angeles, New York Model Mgmt, and Salt Models Atlanta.  

In April 2016, Meredith appeared twice on the cover of Vogue magazine. No doubt Meredith is gorgeous with the height of five feet ten inches and with the balanced diet, she has managed the weight at 60kg.
