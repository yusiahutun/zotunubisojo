Evan Ross Go to Bed with Me Video

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 11, 2024

After his wife and fellow musician, Ashlee Simpson Ross, showed us her nightly skin care routine in the last episode of Go to Bed with Me, Evan Ross is now showing us what he uses to take care of his skin while filming at The West Hollywood EDITION. In addition to his own products, Ross shares an appreciation for particular brands, from La Mer to Nyakio, with his wife.

Shop Evan's Routine

With sensitive skin, Ross makes sure to curate a selection of products that won't irritate his face. He starts off by using The Cleansing Foam by La Mer, a brand his other half also uses in her routine. "It's my wife's favorite, as well as mine now," he says. "She put me onto it." He also mentions that he appreciates the product for how easy and efficient it is.

Next, Ross opts for an exfoliating and cooling mask. "I'm very masculine too, but I do like pretty-looking bottles," Ross says of Dior's Hydra Life Glow Better Fresh Jelly Mask.

He adds that he uses only a small amount when applying it to his face. "I don't use too much because it's Dior and it's a very small package, and no one needs to be buying eight millions of these," he says. "You could tell my wife that."

Watch the video above to see how Ross incorporates the rest of his (and his wife's) favorite beauty products into his skin care routine.
