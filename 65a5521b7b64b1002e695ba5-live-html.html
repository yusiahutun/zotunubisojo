The Best FIFA Football Awards 2023: Winners and highlights

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Good evening everybody and welcome to another MARCA in English live blog, this time taking you through the 'The Best' awards gala which is taking place in London.

Plenty of star names are up for individual awards. Erling Haaland, Kylian Mbappe and Lionel Messi are the final three for the top men's player award.

While Aitana Bonmati, Linda Caicedo and Jennifer Hermoso are the women's candidates.

Players will also have the chance to be named in the FIFPRO World XI, meaning there is plenty of reason to tune in to tonight's ceremony.

This year, the men's awards - and Puskas award - are judged from December 19, 2022 to August 20, 2023, because the Qatar World Cup was included in last year's ceremony.

As for the women's awards, they are based on performances from August 1, 2022 to August 20, 2023.

What time is The Best 2023 awards gala?

The 2023 The Best awards gala will be held in London and will take place on January 15 at 20.30 CET, meaning it is 19:30 in London and 14:30 ET/11:30 PT.

Where to watch The Best Awards 2023 on TV

The 2023 The Best Awards Gala can be watched live on Eurosport and Mega.

You can also watch it online for free from the FIFA+ website, as well as the app available for smart TVs.
