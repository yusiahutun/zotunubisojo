Charlotte Tilbury launches her first fragrance

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 22, 2024

Since she launched her cosmetics range in August 2013, Charlotte Tilbury has dominated the industry with her easy-to-shop, high-performing products. Next month, the make-up artist is set to launch her first fragrance, Scent of a Dream.

Like her other beauty products, this was a no-expense-spared project and it has a point of difference to other scents on the market. "I have been mixing my own 'secret scent' for years and have been fascinated by the idea that one's perfume can attract others and also change the energy frequency of the people and environment around you," explains Tilbury.

The make-up artist worked with neuroscience and fragrance experts to create a memorable perfume that blends floral extracts and musks. There are also notes of amber, lemon and black pepper in the juice.    

Sølve Sundsbø

Tilbury also enlisted the help of her friend Kate Moss to front the campaign. "This is Kate as you've never seen her before; bewitching, beguiling, and entrancing," says Tilbury. "I have known Kate for 25 years now, and have had so many incredible experiences with her… she gets me, and she gets the scent."
