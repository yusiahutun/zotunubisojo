Rhea Ripley, the charismatic wrestler, has been rumoured to be in relationships with several fellow high-profile wrestlers, including Dominik Mysterio and Demetri Jackson. Demi Bennett (Rhea Ripley) is an Australian professional wrestler contracted to WWE. She is associated with The Judgement Day stable in the wrestling world and holds the Women’s World Championship title.

Rhea Ripley and Dominik Mysterio relationship

Is Rhea Ripley in a relationship with Dominik Mysterio? No, Rhea Ripley and Dominik Mysterio aren't romantically involved in real life. Their on-screen relationship is scripted to popularise their team, The Judgement Day, within the wrestling world.

As part of The Judgement Day, Dominik Mysterio has embraced the character of the late Eddie Guerrero alongside Rhea Ripley, who refers to herself as Dominik's "Mami." However, despite fans being aware that Dominik and Rhea's connection is purely scripted, they’re still captivated by it.

Rhea Ripley's husband

Are Rhea Ripley and Dominik Mysterio married? No, Rhea Ripley (real name Demi Bennet) has never been in a real relationship with Dominik Mysterio and isn't married to anyone.

However, she is dating a fellow wrestler, Adams “Buddy” Matthews. Matthew Adams is an Australian professional wrestler contracted with All Elite Wrestling (AEW).

He is a former one-time AEW World Trios Champion and a House of Black stable member. Adams, best known for his WWE time, where he performed under ring names Murphy and Buddy Murphy, also appears on the independent circuit.
