David Spade reveals he gained 25lbs in 'mystery weight' as he chats to Ellen about growing older

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

David Spade talked about how he learned of a 25-pound 'mystery weight' gain during an appearance airing on Monday on The Ellen DeGeneres Show.

The 54-year-old comedian in an excerpt released on Sunday told Ellen, 60, that he normally weighs 140 pounds and after age 30 stopped checking.

David said he underwent a physical for the 2010 comedy Grown Ups and learned he weighed 165 pounds.

Coveted title: David Spade discussed gaining 25 pounds during an appearance on Monday on The Ellen DeGeneres Show

'I put on 25 pounds of mystery weight somewhere on my body,' David said.

David said he talked to his co-star Chris Rock who was not surprised by the weight gain and told him 'everyone's whispering'.

Ellen said that David could earn a Grammy Award for his new memoir A Polaroid Guy In A Snapchat World.

'I'm trying to go for that whole EGOT. ...I don't have any yet. I have an Emmy nomination, that's it I guess,' David quipped referencing his Emmy nomination for Just Shoot Me! in 1999.

There have been only a handful of people who have won all four major entertainment awards, including: Rita Moreno, John Gielgud, Audrey Hepburn, Mel Brooks, Whoopi Goldberg and John Legend.
