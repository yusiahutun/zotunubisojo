Dannii 'to strip for Playboy'

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

The gorgeous X Factor judge first posed for the saucy mag 13 years ago, and now says she would do it again for the right price.

She says: “I did Playboy when it was cool and I shocked myself and many others.

“But it was liberating at the time. I would do it again.”

She added: “If the money was right, then sure. Who knows, it might happen this year too.”

The star's rep has denied the claims, however, saying: “Dannii has no current plans to do Playboy magazine again. She has no regrets either as the pictures were beautiful.”
