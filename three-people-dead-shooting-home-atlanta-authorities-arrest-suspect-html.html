Three people including a man, woman and teen girl, 17, were shot dead inside a home as police arrested a man, 21, and classified the killings as a 'domestic shooting'.

Deputies arrested Jailon Gray, who was at home where he lives when they arrived and now faces three counts of murder.

The man, 51, and woman, 50, together with teenager, were pronounced dead at the scene, police said.

The Rockdale County Sheriff's Office received a report of gunshots at 12.10am in the Honey Creek Country Club neighborhood of Conyers, Georgia, some 30 miles east of Atlanta.

Police say the 'domestic shooting' was triggered by an argument and was not random but have not yet confirmed the relationship between Gray and the victims.

Three other people were inside the home at the time of the shooting. 

The Rockdale County Sheriff's Office received a report of gunshots at 12.10am in the Honey Creek Country Club neighborhood of Conyers, Georgia, some 30 miles (48 kilometers) east of Atlanta, WSB-TV reported

Deputies arrived and found three people who had been shot. The victims were pronounced dead at the scene

A suspect who is yet to be identified was in custody. It is not yet know as to what led to the shooting

Their identities have not yet been released but neighbors told WSB-TV a 17-year-old girl lives at the home with her two parents. 

Gray was still at the house when officers arrived and was arrested without incident. He is charged with three counts of aggravated assault and three of felony murder.

Police have been to the residence before, but did not specify what for.

The investigation is ongoing.
