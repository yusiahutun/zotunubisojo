Survey Shows Which Celebs People Fantasize About the Most Sexually

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 23, 2024

In case you guys haven’t heard, May is Masturbation Month. In honor of this important tribute to self-pleasure, vibrator company We-Vibe released a report about solo sex habits featuring the results of more than 1,000 men and women—and there were some intriguing findings.

A few highlights: Women are 16 times more likely than dudes to use a sex toy during the act, while 91-percent of men say their primary way to get turned on is by using porn—shocking!—which is about 25-percent more than women. Women were also more likely than men to use strategies like new music or doing it in a different room as a way to “spice up” their self-pleasure routines.

But the funniest results, we think, were the ones that divulged which celebrities your average man and woman fantasizes about when getting off solo.

Among the male fantasy subjects, Channing Tatum, Ryan Gosling, Adam Levine, Johnny Depp, and Justin Timberlake came out on top. Jennifer Lopez was the number-one woman—pretty impressive, considering she’s been in the public eye for decades now, but her sex appeal hasn’t waned—with Carrie Underwood, Jennifer Lawrence, Jessica Alba, Emma Watson, Kim Kardashian, Megan Fox, Selena Gomez, and Taylor Swift filling out the top ten.

Ultimately, only 33-percent of men reported using celebs as a mental stimulus, while only 19-percent of women did. Kinda surprising! If women aren’t watching much porn or thinking about many celebs, then who are we thinking about—our partners? Fodder for another survey, we think.
