Below Deck Mediterranean unveils Kiwi-born Chief Stewardess Katie Flood

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, April 12, 2024

Below Deck Mediterranean has dropped the explosive trailer for the show's long-awaited sixth season.

And among the cast is Kiwi-born beauty Katie Flood, who is replacing fan favourite Hannah Ferrier as the new Chief Stewardess this season.

According to Bravo TV, the New Zealand native, 29, is a 'perfectionist' who joined the yachting industry six years ago.
