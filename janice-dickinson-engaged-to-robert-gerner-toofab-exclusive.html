Supermodel Janice Dickinson is Engaged!

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

From supermodel to superbride!

Janice Dickinson is engaged, toofab can exclusively report. The self-proclaimed "World's First Supermodel's" fiance is Dr. Robert Gerner, a Los Angeles-based psychologist.

Janice, 57 and Robert, 67, met on a blind date at Chateau Marmont and have been dating for a few months. The couple is planning to get married in April 2013.

Robert proposed to Janice with a Victorian promise ring; an engagement ring will follow shortly.

Janice has been previously married three times, and has a son and daughter.

"You just know you know it's the right person," the supermodel tells toofab. "He's the ying to my yang."

A housewarming party scheduled for this weekend at the couple's new Hollywood Hills home will also serve as the first of two engagement parties. Invited guests include Karl Lagerfeld, Donatella Versace, Slash and Sandra Bullock.
