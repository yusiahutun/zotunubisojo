MAFS: Evelyn Ellis hints Justin Bieber broke her heart during 2016 fling

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, April 6, 2024

Married At First Sight's Evelyn Ellis has shared new details about her night out with Justin Bieber, after their history together resurfaced this week.

The 26-year-old was working as a nightclub hostess in London when she crossed paths with the Peaches hit-maker, 29, on a night out in February 2016.

Photos from the night showed Justin, Evelyn and several other women clambering into a taxi after leaving London's Tape nightclub in the early hours of the morning.

Evelyn told 2Day FM's Hughesy, Ed & Erin on Wednesday she couldn't 'say too much' about what happened, but it was a 'fun night'.

The intruder MAFS bride also refused to confirm or deny whether Justin had broken her heart.

'This story is too good but I can't say [much], but what I will say is, it was a fun night out,' she told radio hosts Erin Molan, Ed Kavalee and Dave 'Hughesy' Hughes.

'Did he break your heart?' Erin asked.

Evelyn went silent for several seconds before Erin asked a follow-up question: 'Do you have his phone number?'

'I do not deny or confirm these allegations,' she responded.

Sydney-born Evelyn tied the knot with TV 'husband' Rupert Burden on this year's season of Married At First Sight.

Things got off to an awkward start for the pair when the groom dropped their wedding rings at the altar.

'He's dropped the ring!' a guest was heard laughing.

Later, the model and marketing director smiled at her on-screen partner as he struggled to read his vows.

A nervous Rupert could barely get through his speech, as Evelyn patiently watched on.

When it came time to exchange rings, the electrician struggled to find the ring in his blazer pocket. Evelyn later complained: 'He had one job!'
