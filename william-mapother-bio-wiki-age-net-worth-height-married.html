William Mapother Bio

William Mapother was born on 17 April 1965, in Louisville, Kentucky, the USA. As per his wiki, his zodiac sign is Aries. He is the son of William Reibert Mapother, Sr. (father) and Louisa Riehm Mapother (mother). As per nationality, he is American and his ethnicity is mixed (English, Irish and German). His father was an attorney, bankruptcy consultant and judge in Louisville from 1967 to 1970; William Sr., who died on 22 June 2006 after fighting lung cancer and pulmonary fibrosis. In addition, William is the cousin of actor Tom Cruise. 

Concerning about Mapother’s educational background, he graduated from the University of Notre Dame in English. Before joining the film industry, Mapother was a school teacher in East Los Angeles for 3 years.

William Mapother Career

Taking about William Mapother career, he became well known as a character actor, playing scary or otherwise dark characters at times.
