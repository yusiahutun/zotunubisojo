Laguna Beach and The Hills alum Casey Reinhardt marries in lavish beachfront ceremony

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

Laguna Beach castmember and cupcake queen Casey Reinhardt wed her longtime beau Sean Brown in a romantic sunset wedding on Saturday.

The former reality star turned entrepreneur shared photos and video clips on Instagram from the lavish nuptials that were held at an upscale Newport Beach resort overlooking the Pacific Ocean.

'Last night was the most magical, incredible, most emotional night of my life!!!' the 29-year-old wrote alongside a photo of her and Sean kissing as fireworks exploded in the background. 

I do: Former castmember of reality shows Laguna Beach and The Hills Casey Reinhardt tied the knot with Sean Brown on Saturday in Newport Beach, California

She gushed: 'I became Mrs. Casey Beau Brown and I get to spend the rest of my life with the most amazing man who ever walked this earth.'

'I'm so happy, so in love, and so overwhelmed with emotion just thinking about how beautiful the rest of our lives are going to be together.'

The owner of Casey's Cupcakes also shared short video clips of the elaborate fireworks display and the decorated reception that literally dripped with white exotic blooms. 

Luxury location: The couple exchanged their vows at sunset under an outdoor canopy laden with white flowers at an upscale resort

For the ceremony Casey, who also starred on the reality show The Hills, wore a lace gown with a sheer patterned bodice and a tiara and veil.

Her long blonde hair was styled in loose curls that fell down her back.

Her groom, who happens to be the nephew of the late Nicole Brown Simpson, wore a white tuxedo.

No expense spared: The 29-year-old shared a view of the reception which literally dripped with white blooms. 'my dream became my reality last night,' she wrote

Big celebration: The union was marked by an elaborate fireworks display out over the water

Serenaded: Singer Sara Bareilles performed the couple's song I Choose You during the ceremony

Singer Sara Bareilles performed at the wedding, singing the couple's special song I Choose You.

'I cried uncontrollably,' Casey shared with her followers. 'It was so incredibly touching.' 

Once the couple were pronounced husband and wife, the fireworks display ignited over the ocean in celebration

Former reality star: Casey was a castmember of the MTV reality series Laguna Beach: The Real orange County in 2005
