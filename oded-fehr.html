Oded Fehr - Bio, Age, Height, Net Worth, Facts, Nationality

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

Table of Biography

Oded Fehr is a popular Israeli actor based in the United States. Oded Fehr is best known for appearing as Ardeth Bay in “The Mummy” film franchise series, as well as Carlos Olivera in two “Resident Evil” films.

Early Life

Oded Fehr was born on 23 November 1970, in Tel Aviv, Israel. According to the astrological chart, he has a zodiac sign of Sagittarius and is currently 53 years old as of 2024. He belongs to the European Jewish ethnicity and follows the Judaism religion. In addition, he holds an Israeli nationality.

The names of his parents are Uri Fehr and Gila Fehr. His father is a geophysicist, and marketing executive while his mother is a daycare supervisor. In addition, his paternal grandparents were Joachim/Hans Fehr and Miriam Shaltiel. He was raised along with his two siblings, a brother, and a sister as well as he has a younger half-sister.

Education

He went to train at the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School in Bristol, England after taking a very brief drama class in Frankfurt, Germany. From 1989 to 1992, he served as a soldier in the Israeli Navy and used to work security for the Israeli airline El Al.

Professional Career

Oded Fehr made his acting debut with the role of Lukics in the 1994 ITV television crime drama series “The Knock.” Later, he appeared in a couple of miniseries such as “Killer Net” (1998), “Cleopatra” (1999), and “Arabian Nights” (2000).

Moreover, the actor made his big screen debut with the 1998 film “Kiss of Fire.” After appearing in a couple of miniseries, he caught Director Stephen Sommers’s eyes. Moreover, the director cast Oded as Ardeth Bay in “The Mummy” (1999) and its sequel “The Mummy Returns” in 2001.

Caption: Oded Fehr plays the role of Ardeth Bay in the film, The Mummy (Source: Revista Tema Livre)

The film’s success helped him to open the doors in Hollywood and quickly started his other acting projects. Between this year, he played minor roles in movies like “Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo”(1999), “Bread and Roses” (2000), and “Texas Rangers” (2000).

He was cast in the 2004 action horror movie “Resident Evil: Apocalypse” as Carlos Oliveira, the second main character in the computer game “Resident Evil.” He then appeared in its sequels “Resident Evil: Extinction” (2007) and “Resident Evil: Retribution (2012).

Recent Works and Voice roles

He portrayed the most powerful demon, Zankou, in the seventh season of the fantasy drama TV series “Charmed.” His subsequent films include the drama film “The Betrayed” (2008), the horror film “Super Hybrid” (2012), and – as minor characters – the science fiction television series “V” and the fantasy drama television series “Once Upon a Time.”

In 2018, he played the role of Eitan Hafri in the American-British science fiction TV series “The First.” That same year, he played the role of Daran/Narek Zakarian in the 2018 British science-fiction horror film “White Chamber.” Between the years 2019-2021, he appeared in TV series such as “Blood & Treasure” “Younger” (2019), and “FBI: Most Wanted, FBI and FBI: International.”

In addition to acting, the Israeli is a voice actor. He enjoys the DC Comics universe. He lent voice to Doctor Fate in “Justice League Unlimited” (2003-2005), Equinox in “Batman: The Brave and the Bold” (2009-2011), and Ra’s al Ghul in “Young Justice” (2011-2013).

He has also been the voice of Osiris in the video game “Destiny 2” since its Curse of Osiris expansion in 2017. Moreover, he has played Fleet Admiral Charles Vance on “Star Trek: Discovery” since 2020.
