Emmerdale fans 'expose' Caleb as they uncover link to drug dealer Callum after clue

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 12, 2024

Emmerdale fans believe they have finally uncovered Caleb Dingle's true motive - he is somehow linked to drug dealer Callum (Tom Ashley).

Viewers have been wary of the the long lost half-brother of Cain (Jeff Hordley) and Chas Dingle (Lucy Pargeter), since he arrived on Christmas Day and have continued speculating what he is hiding.

After her close friend Suzy Merton (Martelle Edinborough) was attacked by Callum with a brick, Leyla Harding (Roxy Shahidi) broke down in the graveyard when she was discovered by Caleb.

This led to a her arranging a meeting with the dealer, but after paying him the money he'd been demanding, Leyla's world was turned upside down when Callum grabbed her.

Kicking and screaming, Leyla was forced into the boot of his car before he tied her up and gagged her in a secluded shed.

Given that it was Caleb's advice that led Leyla into danger, fans have speculated that he could be working with Callum.

Taking to social media with their theories, one person penned: "I don’t know why but anyone else think Callum’s boss is Caleb? #Emmerdale."

"I’m wondering if Caleb is the boss of Callum? “He’s not to be messed with” Caleb can be very sneaky. Just my opinion #Emmerdale," added a second.

Another viewer questioned: "I wonder if Caleb knows Callum?"

Echoing this, someone else wrote: "Just a thought is Caleb involved with Callum is that why he’s suddenly giving leyla advice & whatever".

Sharing their thoughts on Caleb's true identity, a fifth tweeted: "I think Caleb is Callum’s boss. All his money comes from drugs. He’s a drug lord and l think he knows about Will’s past that’s why he antagonises him and why he’s gone into business with Kim."

Other theories include Caleb being an undercover police officer or having links to other residents, including Alex Moore (Liam Boyle) or bent copper Mark Malone (Mark Womack).
