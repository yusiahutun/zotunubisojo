Celebrity Kids Make Rare Appearances With Hollywood Parents: Photos

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, April 12, 2024

One of the best parts about keeping up with Hollywood stars is being able to keep up with their celebrity kids too! Some like Chrissy Teigen‘s children, Luna and Miles, as well as Eva Longoria‘s son, Santiago, are adorably plastered all over the internet, while other parents like Blake Lively and Jennifer Garner try their best to shield their kiddos from the spotlight. However, every now and then, fans are lucky enough to get a quick glimpse into the lives of these celebrity children.

Although they’re still young, it seems Chrissy and husband John Legend‘s little ones are already adapting to life in the limelight. The Cravings author revealed her eldest daughter is already taking after her wit and humor as a toddler.

“Luna has amazing timing,” the Chrissy’s Court star told Entertainment Tonight in June 2020. “She is so verbal … obviously, everyone’s kids say something and you’re like, ‘Where did you get that?’ But with us, it’s like, ‘How did you learn that tone or phrasing?’ She phrases things really well and she holds a beat to give the punchline with things. It’s really interesting. She’s so funny.”

Even Eva can’t believe how quickly her little man is growing up. While celebrating Santiago’s 3nd birthday in June 2021, the Desperate Housewives star gushed over her bond with her little bundle of joy. “I’m so lucky to be your mama and I’m so happy you chose me to be with you through this life,” she gushed. “Your laughter makes my heart explode and your kisses warm my soul! I love you, papa!”

Other celeb moms and dads like Kate Hudson and Sofia Vergara have no qualms about posting photos of their family on social media, but Jennifer had a good reason for not wanting to give fans glimpses inside her life as the mom of Violet, Seraphina and Samuel.

“It’s so gnarly to be a kid now. I guard my kids’ privacy as much as I possibly can, and I’ve never posted pictures of them on Instagram,” the 13 Going on 30 star explained to InStyle in August 2019. “I used to refuse to say their names during interviews — but everyone knows their names! I would just say ‘my eldest,’ which I still do out of habit a lot.”
