A lost bet game is that game which has not gone your way. For instance, when you and your friend or loved one take bets, one of you will end up a loser. It is usually done to while time and have fun.

  • Challenge each other to see who can cook the best dish.
  • Have a contest to see who can go without talking for the longest period of time.

  • Make a prediction about things a family member will do at the next family gathering.
