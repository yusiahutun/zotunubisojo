The best hair accessories can take your look from basic to stunning in no time. From fancy hairpins to boho-chic headbands, these small embellishments can have a big impact on your overall appearance. Not only can they add an extra touch of style to any outfit, but they can also serve practical purposes like holding back unruly hair or providing extra volume.

Our contributor William Slator, Product developer at HairGuard, a holistic hair care product brand, says, “When it comes to securing updos, you want a secure hair accessory that doesn’t put pressure on the hair strands while making your hairstyle look attractive. This is important for reducing hair breakage and split ends. That’s why I recommend silk scrunchies for buns and more sophisticated updos, and alligator and claw clips for more casual looks. Silk scrunchies slide on the hair and alligator clips distribute the weight so no one area is being pulled too tightly.”

Whether you're dressing up for a special event or just want to add some flair to your everyday style, these cute accessories for hair are essential additions to your beauty arsenal.

Our contributor Nate Masterson, Hair Expert for Maple Holistics, says, “There are a wide range of hair accessories such as hairpins, clips, scrunchies, headbands, bobby pins and claw clips, that people can use to amp up their hair game.     Let’s understand how to choose the best hair accessories for your requirements.

    How to Choose the Best Hair Accessories?

    Picking the right hair accessories is just as crucial as selecting the perfect outfit or makeup. They can enhance your overall appearance and give a touch of personality and style to your look. Here are a few guidelines to help you in making the right choice:

    1. Know Your Hair Type

    Different types of hair require different kinds of hair accessories. For instance, certain accessories work best with thick hair while others are designed for thin hair. Therefore, understanding your hair type is essential in making the right choice. 

    2. Consider the Occasion

    Not every accessory is suitable for every occasion. Some accessories are casual, while others are more formal. Choose an accessory that matches the event. 

    3. Choose the Right Size

    The size of the hair accessory is critical. A large accessory can overwhelm your features, while a too-small one may not be noticeable. Choose a size that complements your facial features and fits comfortably in your hair.

    4. Pick the Right Color

    Select a color that compliments your hair shade and skin tone. If you have darker hair, light-colored accessories can provide a nice contrast and vice versa.

    5. Comfort Is Key

    While style is important, comfort should not be overlooked. Choose hair accessories that are comfortable to wear and do not cause any discomfort or damage to your hair.

    6. Match Your Outfit

    The right hair accessory can complete your look. Try to match the accessory with your outfit or make it a contrasting statement piece. 

    7. Quality Matters

    High-quality hair accessories last longer and look better. Although they may be more expensive, they often provide a better value for your money in the long run.

    Remember, the best hair accessory for you is the one that you feel most confident and comfortable wearing. Explore various styles, shapes, and colors to discover the perfect pieces for you.

    Sharing the importance of maintaining hair accessories, our contributor William Slator says, “You trust your shampoo to cleanse your scalp and locks, so why not your hair accessories? From bobby pins and claw clips to scrunchies and even hair bands, you can use warm water and shampoo to keep your hair accessories clean and well-maintained. Just fill a sink or bowl with warm water and a few drops of shampoo and submerge your hair accessories for a few minutes. Remove, rinse, and lay flat to dry. In a pinch, you can spritz your hard hair accessories, like bobby pins and clips, with rubbing alcohol.”

    FAQ's

    Are there eco-friendly hair accessories available in the market?

    Yes, there are eco-friendly hair accessories available in the market. Many brands offer sustainable options made from materials like organic cotton, bamboo, or recycled plastics, reducing their environmental impact.

    Can I prevent hair damage when using hair accessories?

    You can prevent hair damage when using hair accessories by choosing options that are gentle on your hair, such as soft scrunchies or fabric-covered hair bands. Additionally, avoid overly tight accessories and be mindful of how you remove them to minimize hair breakage.

    Which hair accessories work best for short hair?

    Hair accessories for short hair can include bobby pins, snap clips, or small barrettes. These are great for securing shorter strands and adding a touch of style to your hair.

    Are there hair accessories suitable for children?

    Yes, there are hair accessories suitable for children. Kid-friendly options like colorful, soft scrunchies, snap clips, or headbands are designed to be comfortable and safe for children to wear.

    Can hair accessories be used for different hairstyles?

    Hair accessories can indeed be used for different hairstyles. Depending on the type of accessory, you can create various looks, from updos to loose waves, and everything in between.

    Can I use hair accessories to add volume to my hair?

    Yes, hair accessories can be used to add volume to your hair. Options like hair clips, combs, or claw clips can be strategically placed to lift and volumize your hair, creating a fuller look. Additionally, using accessories like scrunchies in a messy bun or ponytail can give the appearance of more volume.
