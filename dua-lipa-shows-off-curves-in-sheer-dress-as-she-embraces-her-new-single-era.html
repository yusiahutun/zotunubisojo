Dua Lipa shows off curves in sheer dress as she embraces her new 'single era'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Dua Lipa took to the Red Carpet at a star-studded 3rd Annual Academy Museum Gala in Los Angeles this Sunday, and all eyes were on her as she stunned in a see-through black dress with a cheeky display.

The music star is reportedly said to have split up with her boyfriend, Romain Gavras after a whirlwind eight-month romance. So it seems like Dua Lipa is in her single era, and we’re here for it.

Dua Lipa leaves little to the imagination with see-through black dress

The One Kiss singer opted for a floor-length lacy black gown, which was fitted enough to show off her toned figure.

The lacy ensemble was also see-through, as the star gave audiences a cheeky glimpse of her black underwear.

Adding to the already stunning detail, the gown also came with a backless design, mostly covered by her new red hair, styled into effortless waves.

Although the dress gave off enough glam on its own, the singer jazzed up the look with diamond-encrusted earrings and necklace.

It seems Dua is a big fan of the sheer look, sporting an unforgettable sparkly sheer dress at the Barbie premiere earlier this year.

The star-studded event was also attended by celebrities including Kendall Jenner and Selena Gomez.

The show-stopping dress comes after it was reported the music star split from her boyfriend, as sources claimed the singer would be focusing on her music into the new year.

“Dua and Romain have gone their separate ways after a summer of love,” a source told The Sun.

Fans have divided opinions on the gown – and some are concerned

Although many fans were in love with the look, others weren’t too sure, as they took to X, formerly known as Twitter, to discuss.

Others seemed to be concerned about the 28-year-old, commenting on how she “looked sad” after her reported breakup.

“Where did her old stylist go??” questioned one disappointed fan.

“I see the vision but the execution….” tweeted another.

One concerned fan tweeted: “She looks sad…maybe that’s just me projecting. Idk tho…I’m picking up some vibes.”

However, others disagreed as one simply wrote: “GORGGG.”

“This dress is givingggg,” wrote another.
