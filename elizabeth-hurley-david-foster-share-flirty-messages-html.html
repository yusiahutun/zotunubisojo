Elizabeth Hurley and David Foster share flirty messages

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, March 14, 2024

David Foster and Elizabeth Hurley are taking their new relationship public.

On the same day that photos showed the pair getting out of Italy following a weekend spent on the $80 million yacht of one of David's billionaire friends, the two both posted messages on Instagram.

David, 67, commented on a snap of Hurley, 52, posing in her bathing suit and pair of flippers by writing 'mermaid.'

Meanwhile, the first photos of the pair together over the weekend have emerged courtesy of the wife of billionaire media proprietor Haim Saban, who was also on the trip.

Cheryl Saban shared photos of David and Liz posing together with the other guests on the yacht, and enjoying a quiet moment on the ship's deck, writing: 'Sun, fun, food, friends. #itsallgood.'

Hurley also enjoyed a playful exchange with David's daughter Sara, who is married to German tennis star Tommy Haas.

After Sara posted a video of herself dancing at the club Billionaire in Porto Cervo with the caption 'goes to a club once,' Hurley commented: 'Must have been after we left.'

She then added a winking face emoji.

Sara responded to Hurley by writing: 'About an hour after.'

To that she added a rock n' roll emoji along with three hearts.

It was also on Instagram that Hurley thanked host Tamar Mounikan by tagging her in an Instagram post which showed her posing in a Coco Fennell dress on the deck of the yacht.

The star of 'The Royals' let Cheryl Saban how much she enjoyed meeting the group on Instagram as well, by commenting on a video that showed the group enjoying a private serenade courtesy of Andrea Bocelli. 

'So happy to have met you both xxxx,' wrote Hurley. 

Vatche 'Bob' Mounikan, who is a founding partner at HSBC Bank and the owner of the Dorchester Hotel in London, splashed out $80 million in 1992 to buy his megayacht, which he named 'Siran.'

He then did an extensive remodeling job on the vessel in 2015, extending the length of the vessel 10 feet.

Previous videos posted by Sara and her sister Erin to their always amusing Instagram accounts revealed that the two were with their father David at Billionaire that night. 

Given the frantic scene inside the club, which was pitch black save for the occasional passing bottle with sparkler attached, it was hard to see in those videos if Hurley was with David. 

Most of those videos were posted by Erin, who was joined at the club by jewelry designer Jennifer Meyer and Dasha Zhukova, the wife of Russia billionaire Roman Abramovich.

The 'Barely Famous' stars are two of David's three daughters from his second marriage to Rebecca Dyer.

His third daughter, New York-based stylist Jordan Foster-Woodger, also joined her siblings on the trip.

Foster divorced Dyer the same year that she gave birth to Jordan, and in 1991 married Caitlyn Jenner's ex Linda Thompson.

That union ended in 2005, and six years later Foster married his fourth and most recent wife, Yolanda Hadid.

Hadid is set to share her thoughts on the collapse of her marriage to David in her upcoming memoir, 'Believe Me.'

She will also detail how she claims to have changed the man, calling him a 'diamond in the rough' that needed polishing.'

'I put him on a healthy diet, helped him lose twenty-five pounds, redid his wardrobe, and never missed one of his doctors' appointments,' writes Hadid in the book, set for release this September.

Erin and Sara already seem far more fond of their father's new lady friend than they were of their former stepmother judging by their comments about Hadid in a 2016 Sirius XM interview.

'Only she knows her journey, but what I will say is if you're capable of continuing to be on a reality show, you're not dying. Let's be honest,' Sara said of the former 'Real Housewives' star while appearing on 'The Jenny McCarthy Show!'.

Erin then quickly interjected: 'Again, we are not doctors. We have no idea what goes on, and we don't wish anyone ill will of any kind.' 

She went on to say: 'However, I think that from everyone's experience, if anyone's ever known someone who is very sick, who has cancer, who is dying, who's in the last year of their life, the only thing that person gives a s**t about is getting through the day and being treated normally.

'No one wants everyone to know how sick they are and everyone to see how much they're struggling, and when that seems to be the focus, making everyone see how sick you are, that's just confusing to someone who's trying to be supportive.' 

Hurley has had her fair share of high-profile relationships as well, spending over a decade on the arm of Hugh Grant.

It was her appearance alongside the British thespian at the 1994 premiere of 'Four Weddings and a Funeral' that shot her to fame thanks to her her barely-pinned Versace dress, which earned her millions of admirers and an Estee Lauder contract.

The two split in 2000 however but remain friends, and over the next three years Hurley was rumored to be with a number of men, most notably with Stephen Bing.

In 2002, Hurley gave birth to a son Damian with the billionaire businessman.

Her first and only marriage was in 2007 to Indian businessman Arun Nayar, with the two divorcing four years later.

Hurley's most recent relationship was with cricket player Shane Warren.
