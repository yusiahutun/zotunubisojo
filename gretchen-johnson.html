Gretchen Johnson- Second Wife of The Late Family Feud Host, Richard Dawson

Most of the 80s Family Feud viewers must be familiar with the funniest and most charming host Richard Dawson. But little does anyone know about his second wife. So, let's shed light upon his beloved beautiful wife, Gretchen Johnson. 

Gretchen Johnson tied the knot with her husband Richard Dawson.  SOURCE: Pinterest

Well, Gretchen walked down the aisle with her boyfriend turned husband Richard Dawson in 1991 at their intimate ceremony. The lovebirds met for the first time during an episode of Family Feud back in 1981. There 26 years old Gretchen participated with her family as a contestant and instantly caught Richard's eye.

Moreover, it was love at first sight for the host. At the end of the show, Richard went to ask for her phone number & even got it. Then the duo began to know each other & eventually romance was sparked. Dawson also invited her to an in-house dinner party, which both enjoyed a lot.

The lovebirds had a seriously intimate relationship & welcomed a baby girl Shannon Nicole Dawson in 1990, a year before their nuptial. The duo's daughter, Shannon followed in the footsteps of her late father and is an actress and model.

Gretchen Johnson with his husband Richard Dawson and daughter Shanon Dawson. SOURCE: Pinterest

Unfortunately, Gretenchen's married life came to an end with the demise of her husband at the age of 79. Until 61 years of age, Richard smoked 4 packs of cigarettes a day which caused him esophagus cancer. Due to complications of cancer, Dawson lost his life at the Ronald Raegan UCLA Medical Center On 7 June 2012.

Gretchen Johnson's Late Husband Richard Dawson Divorced Actress Diana Dors

Richard Dawson previously dated the blonde British actress & model Diana Dors whom he met at the film studio. After living together for several years, the two tied the knot on 12 April 1959 in New York. They went on to welcome two sons Mark Dawson in 1960 and Gary Dawson, in 1962. 

Richard Dawson together with blonde actress Diana Dors. SOURCE: Pinterest

But, Diana filed for divorce against Richard after having a serious problem in their marriage. Soon the divorce was finalized in 1966, and they both parted ways. Richard moved on with his second wife, Gretchen Johnson, in 1991, whereas Diana exchanged vows with Alan Lake in 1968. Following the separation, Richard took full custody of both kids. Sadly on 4 May 1984, Diana passed away due to ovarian cancer at the age of 52.
