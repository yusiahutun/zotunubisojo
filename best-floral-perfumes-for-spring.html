The 10 Best Floral Perfumes for Spring and Summer 2022

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, May 6, 2024

1

Gucci Bloom Eau de Parfum

1

Gucci Bloom Eau de Parfum

Inspired by a thriving garden with tuberose and jasmine, Gucci's iconic Bloom is the perfect springtime scent.

2

Valentino Voce Viva Eau de Parfum

2

Valentino Voce Viva Eau de Parfum

Now 15% Off

This scent mixes florals like gardenia and orange blossom with crisp citrus notes over a woody, vanilla-centered base.

3

Christian Dior Miss Dior Blooming Bouquet Eau de Toilette

3

Christian Dior Miss Dior Blooming Bouquet Eau de Toilette

With a name like Blooming Bouquet, you can guess what this fan-favorite Miss Dior iteration smells like: Rose, peony, and white musk combine to create a light, airy scent.

Marc Jacobs Daisy Eau So Intense Eau de Parfum

4

Marc Jacobs Daisy Eau So Intense Eau de Parfum

You're probably familiar with the classic fruity-floral Marc Jacobs scent, Daisy, now available in a more intense version that's perfect for long summer days and nights. 

5

Tom Ford Private Blend Rose Prick Eau de Parfum

5

Tom Ford Private Blend Rose Prick Eau de Parfum

Now 15% Off

Two kinds of rose get the Tom Ford treatment with the addition of patchouli and pepper. Sexy and unexpected, this rose perfume is anything but old-fashioned.

6

Viktor&Rolf Flowerbomb

6

Viktor&Rolf Flowerbomb

Now 11% Off

One of the best-selling perfumes of all time, Flowerbomb is a sweet and spicy gourmand floral scent with notes of jasmine, orange blossom, vanilla, and patchouli.

Henry Rose Jake's House

Clean Pick

Henry Rose Jake's House

Versace Dylan Turquoise Pour Femme

Versace Dylan Turquoise Pour Femme

This perfume hits all the right notes: citrus, fruit, floral, and woody. It smells like sunshine in a bottle.
