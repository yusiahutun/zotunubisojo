Loewe launches special 'I Told Ya' collection

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 14, 2024

With Zendaya and Jonathan Anderson on board, it is hardly a surprise that Challengers is getting everyone in fashion so excited, whether it's because of the actress' tennis-adorned stilettos on the red carpet or her sporty Loewe ensembles in the film. But, the piece that has everyone talking the most is actually a slogan T-shirt, one that Anderson designed based off something once worn by John F Kennedy Jr., and which has already been worn by a number of the movie's stars and the designer on the red carpet.

The 'I Told Ya' T-shirt, which is likely to be one of the hits of the summer, was designed by Anderson, who took inspiration from a picture of Kennedy playing frisbee with his dog in the Nineties.

Daniele Venturelli//Getty Images

The design was replicated for Zendaya's character in the film and has been worn by the actress during the press tour, while co-star Josh O'Connor wore a shirt version on the red carpet, and Anderson – who is responsible for the film's costumes – posed in one alongside director Luca Guadagnino as they promoted the film in Rome.

Now, Loewe has announced the launch of a line of 'I Told Ya' T-shirts and sweatshirts, which will no doubt be a sell-out success. The label has released both white and grey iterations of the style in lightweight cotton jersey, which are unisex, and will look excellent worn oversized with jeans and loafers all summer long.
