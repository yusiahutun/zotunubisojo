Lisa Vanderpump and Lala Kent sizzle as they attend the Vanderpump Dog Foundation Gala

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Lisa Vanderpump hosted her 5th Annual Vanderpump Dog Foundation Gala on Thursday at The Maybourne in Beverly Hills.

The reality TV mogul, 62, was joined by a number of famous friends, including her Vanderpump Rules co-stars Lala Kent, 32, and James Kennedy, 31.

The ladies brought their fashion A-game to the event, with the former Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star looking ethereal in a floor-length plunging ivory gown that showed off her ample cleavage. 

Meanwhile Kent - who was a prominent figure in Wednesday night's explosive Vanderpump Rules finale - was red hot in a fiery sequin STELLO gown with large cut-outs that showed off her cleavage. 

The event raises funds for pups and help dogs find their forever homes. The Vanderpump Dog Foundation's Rescue Center and Nursery takes care of dogs in need, often pregnant moms, and treats them so they can find loving homes.
