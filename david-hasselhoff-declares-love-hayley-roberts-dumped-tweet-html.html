Just hours after David Hasselhoff tweeted that his romance with Hayley Roberts was no longer, he deleted his post and said all is hunkydory.

The Baywatch star wrote a message on Twitter saying his girlfriend had called things off, before claiming it's 'not true' and they're 'doing great'.

Even Hayley's mother waged in on the confusion, laughing off rumours and claiming her daughter is 'very much in love' with the Hoff.

This was swiftly removed, with various adoring declarations of love and a statement saying: 'Sorry guys not true Me and Hayley are doing Great!!'

During the six months they have been dating they have been inseparable and were seen out together looking more in love than ever just last night.

The pair clasped hands as they were seen out in London at the Clinton's Celebrity Fastcard event.

It seemed that Hasselhoff only had eyes for his blonde bombshell as the pair posed up together.

He even ignored the scantily clad models who were dressed in the famous red lifeguard uniforms.

Dumped: The Baywatch star posted to his Twitter page earlier today

Hayley's mother Lynne Roberts said: 'I speak to Hayley most days, if there was anything wrong she would have told me by now.

'They are happy and get on well, I would say they are getting stronger if anything.'

It was recently claimed that David isn't the first famous face that Hayley has bagged and the 31-year-old reportedly enjoyed a fling with footballer Dwight Yorke and singer Enrique Iglesias too, the Star on Sunday claimed.

The Welsh beauty apparently enjoyed a four-month fling with Dwight that ended weeks before she met the Knight Rider star.

David is said to have proposed to the former Debenhams worker three times, but last night her ring finger appeared bare.

Last month David, who has two daughters from a previous marriage, hinted that he was ready to get married for a third time.

He admitted: 'I believe in marriage. Right now with Hayley things are getting better in every way.'

No ring yet? Hayley's ring finger was noticeably bare, despite the Hoff having proposed three times in recent weeks

United front: David only had eyes for Hayley at the event with the pair holding hands for most of the evening but today he said she had dumped him

And it seems that David was planning a Christmas with Hayley and her family and is appearing in a panto in Bristol, close to Hayley's hometown of Glynneath, near Merthyr Tydfil.

The Hoff has also recently splashed out on a new home in Lisvane, an upmarket area in the Welsh capital of Cardiff.

The Hoff, 59, has been dating the Roberts for the past six months after she asked him for an autograph and he asked her for a date.

The real Baywatch beauty: The Hoff puts a protective arm around his girlfriend Hayley as they leave the party

Old flame: David was married to Pamela Bach until 2006

And it seemed that despite the 28-year age gap, their relationship was blossoming with Hayley even leaving her job in Debenhams to accompany David around the world.

Britain's Got Talent judge Hasselhoff explained their first meeting and told The Sunday Mirror: 'I was walking into the St David's Hotel in Cardiff when we were in Wales for BGT and she came up and asked for a photograph.

'I just saw this girl walk out the bar and I just pointed and went, "her, wow."'

'Hayley's opening line was, "can I have a photo?" And mine was, "only if I can have your number." I then said, "you probably have a boyfriend". And when she said no, that was a done deal.'

Father-of-two Hasselhoff, who divorced wife Pamela Bach in 2006, said: 'I've been able to take my beautiful girlfriend out of a retail store where she's making £5.90 an hour.

'Now she doesn't have to worry. I say look, "you're a beautiful girl, follow what God gave you" and I'm giving her the confidence and have somebody to share my life with.'
