The Best Le Labo Perfumes to Shop in 2024

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Image courtesy of Le Labo

Most Viral Scent

Le Labo Santal 33 Eau de Parfum

Most Viral Scent

Le Labo Santal 33 Eau de Parfum

Credit: Nordstrom

Le Labo's violet-, iris-, and leather-scented Santal 33 is viral for a reason, and also happens to be one of BAZAAR editors' top picks for date night. Contributing beauty director Genevieve Monsma previously said the fragrance’s woody earthiness,"Demands to be noticed, yet manages not to be cloying. Its appeal lies in the fact that the scent is sensual and warm, but understated, with a vibe that’s more crackling fire and cashmere PJs than high heels and red lipstick."

Top:Violet accord
Heart:Iris
Base:Leather

Best Earthy Scent

Le Labo Baie 19 Eau de Parfum

Best Earthy Scent

Le Labo Baie 19 Eau de Parfum

Credit: Saks Fifth Avenue

Anyone who doesn't want their perfume walking into a room before they do can probably agree that Baie 19 serves as the perfect base layer of scent. It's notes of juniper berry and patchouli are inspired by petrichor, or the wet, drenched effect water gives the Earth after it rains.

Top:Juniper berry
Heart:Patchouli
Base:Green leaves

Best Rose Scent

Le Labo Rose 31 Eau de Parfum

Best Rose Scent

Le Labo Rose 31 Eau de Parfum

Credit: Nordstrom

This isn't your average rose perfume: Rose 31 is uniquely powered by the Grasse rose, and balanced with warm, spicy notes of cumin, cedar, and a hint of amber so it's not overly sweet.

Top:Rose absolute
Heart:Cumin
Base:Amber

Best Woody Scent

Le Labo Thé Noir 29 Eau de Parfum

Best Woody Scent

Le Labo Thé Noir 29 Eau de Parfum

Credit: Nordstrom

The woody, earthy scent of Thé Noir 29 is intended as an ode to the craft of tea making. It contains a robust balance of musky, citrus-y notes that linger on the skin for hours.

One satisfied customer writes:

"OK, wow. I received a sample in the mail today and I wanted to write a review as I am getting ready to purchase the full-size bottle. I am very picky when it comes to finding a perfume that I actually love because even some of the biggest name perfumes turn me off because of the overpowering alcohol scent that never goes away after applying. Noir 29 has such a complex woody yet fresh scent that I can’t describe besides saying it smells like I’m having dinner at sunset on the beach during early autumn. That’s it. It’s that good." —Michelle97

Top:Black tea
Heart:Tobacco
Base:Musk

Best Subtle Scent

Le Labo Thé Matcha 26 Eau de Parfum

Best Subtle Scent

Le Labo Thé Matcha 26 Eau de Parfum

Credit: Nordstrom

Similar to Baie 19, Thé Matcha 26 is made with proximity in mind, and features a soft blend of notes like matcha tea accord and vetiver that are best enjoyed up close.

One satisfied customer writes:

"This is one of my favorite scents from Le Labo! It’s a every day scent that lasts all day." —Lysa

Best Floral Scent

Le Labo Lys 41 Eau de Parfum

Best Floral Scent

Le Labo Lys 41 Eau de Parfum

Credit: Nordstrom

We're big fans of Rose 31, but Le Labo's Lys 41 is probably our overall favorite floral fragrance from the brand. It combines jasmine, tuberose, and lily for a vibrant scent that works all year round.

One satisfied customer writes:

"I love this scent! It’s fresh and floral but not overwhelming. It settles on the skin so nicely. I get complements on how this smells every time I wear it." —eayres

Top:Tiare flower
Heart:Jasmine
Base:Madagascar vanilla

Best Vanilla Scent

Le Labo Tonka 25 Eau de Parfum

Best Vanilla Scent

Le Labo Tonka 25 Eau de Parfum

Credit: Nordstrom

Ideal for vanilla perfume enthusiasts everywhere, Tonka 25's warm, spicy, and slightly sweet olfactory blend might become part of your holy-grail fragrance list.

One satisfied customer writes:

"I love that I keep discovering new favorites from Le Labo. First love was Bergamote 22, then Thé Noir 29, then Another 13, and now Tonka 25. And let me just say… it is so sensual, so warm, so cozy. Perfect for daytime and nighttime. This might be my new signature scent." —CountsJa

Top:Absolute tonka
Heart:Orange flower absolute
Base:Musk

Best Fresh Scent

Le Labo Bergamote 22 Eau de Parfum

Best Fresh Scent

Le Labo Bergamote 22 Eau de Parfum

Credit: Net-A-Porter

Here's a pick for those who love using fragrance as mood booster: Bergamote 22 contains fresh notes of grapefruit and bergamot that give way to musk and amber once it dries down.

Top:Bergamot
Heart:Grapefruit
Base:Amber

Best Musky Scent

Le Labo Another 13 Eau de Parfum

Best Musky Scent

Le Labo Another 13 Eau de Parfum

Credit: Saks Fifth Avenue

This popular Le Labo fragrance was commissioned by AnOther Magazine in 2010 as an exclusive scent in partnership with the publication. The unique musk scent has since become one of the beauty brand's most popular perfumes—which actually contains 13 ingredients.

One satisfied customer writes:

"Love this so much!!! My everyday perfume <3" —Michelle F.

Top:Ambroxan (a synthetic animal musk)
Heart:Jasmine
Base:Moss
