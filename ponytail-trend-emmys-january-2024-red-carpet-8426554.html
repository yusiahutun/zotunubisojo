High-Slung Ponytails Were All Over the 2024 Emmys Red Carpet

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, March 14, 2024

The beauty looks at the Emmys were all about drama. The bouffants were voluminous, the blow-outs sleek, and the updos high. We, particularly, can't get enough of the show-stopping ponytails. We saw elevated takes on the classic look with lots of length and body.

"The ponytail is sleek, it's easy, it's chic — but I want some drama," says Tym Wallace, the hairstylist behind Taraji P. Henson's down-to-there pony. "So the drama is the length. That's the added surprise."

This is a stark contrast to the Golden Globes red carpet where the short-and-sweet bob haircut reigned supreme. Ahead, see all the dramatic ponytails that took center stage at the January 2024 Emmy Awards.

Taraji P. Henson

Wallace styled Henson's ponytail using a mix of hair products from the star's own hair-care line, TPH by Taraji, and L'Oréal. To add dramatic length, Wallace added extensions from Hidden Crown sleeked out with the ghd Chronos flat iron.

Tracee Ellis Ross

Tracee Ellis Ross sported a 1960s-inspired high ponytail, with her lengths swept up and away from her face and the ends of the ponytail flipped under.

Laverne Cox

Laverne Cox, who hosted the E! News Emmy's red carpet, wore a powerful ponytail. Created by hairstylist Kiyah Wright, the look featured Cox's rooted blonde hair snatched back into a sleek, high ponytail that cascaded down in pretty loose waves.

Zuri Hall

Zuri Hall's look features a side part with sleek scalloped edges that give way to an voluminous curly pony tail that fades from Hall's natural brunette to a warm caramel hue. The look was created by hairstylist David Robert using extensions from Glam Seamless.

Juliette Lewis

Yellow Jacket's Juliette Lewis donned a high ponytail with a braided detail at the base of the updo. The look was created by hairstylist Paul Norton.
