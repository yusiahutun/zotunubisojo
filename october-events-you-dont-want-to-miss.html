October Events You Dont Want To Miss

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, March 24, 2024


Cocktail Par-tee and Golf Tournament

Experience international foods, music and entertainment on Oct. 13 during the Cocktail Par-tee and Golf Tournament at Legacy Golf and Tennis Club. The event benefits LifeBuilders of Treasure Coast, an organization that helps people progressing through the criminal justice or dependency court systems. (Tickets from $50; lifebuilderstc.com)

Indian River Lagoon Science Festival

Learn all about STEM (science, technology, engineerings and math) and take part in hands-on activities during the fourth annual Indian River Lagoon Science Festival, taking place Oct. 14 at Veterans Memorial Park. (free; irlsciencefest.org)

Oktoberfest Street Festival

Break out the lederhosen and dirndl, and celebrate Oktoberfest on Oct. 7 at Sailfish Brewing Company. Listen to live music, enter the contest for best dressed and enjoy authentic German food, beers and games. ($50; sailfishbrewingco.com)

Mutt March  

Bring your four-legged friend to the Mutt March on Oct. 14 at Memorial Park in support of the Humane Society of Treasure Coast. Join other pet-passionate people and enter your dog in the pet costume contest, and enjoy fall festivities, mini derby races and a pet photo booth. (Tickets from $20; hstcmuttmarch.org)

Indiantown Rodeo

Grab your cowboy hat and boots­—it’s time for a rodeo. Head over to the Indiantown Rodeo at Timer Powers Park on Oct. 13 and 14. Celebrate the rodeo’s 70th year, and witness bareback and saddle bronco riding, steer wrestling and team roping. (Tickets from $18; indiantownrodeo.com)

Taste of Martin County

The Stuart Jet Center will host The Big Taste of Martin County, benefiting Big Brothers Big Sisters of Palm Beach and Martin Counties on Oct. 10. Attendees will sample foods from more than 30 local restaurants and wines from all over the world. There will also be auction prizes and a 50/50 raffle. (Tickets from $45; mentorbig.org)
