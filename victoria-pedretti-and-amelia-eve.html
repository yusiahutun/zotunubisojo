About

Victoria Pedretti is a 28 year old American Actress. Born on 23rd March, 1995 in Fairless Hills, Pennsylvania, U.S., she is famous for Eleanor "Nell" Crain in The Haunting of Hill House, Lulu in Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. Her zodiac sign is Aries.

Amelia Eve is a 31 year old British Actress. Born Amelia McClelland on 2nd February, 1992 in London, Greater London, England, UK, she is famous for her role as Jamie in the Netflix original supernatural-horror series The Haunting of Bly Manor. Her zodiac sign is Aquarius.
