Meghan Markle jams at Taylor Swift concert while her 'London Boy' Harry is overseas

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Meghan Markle took time out of her busy schedule to attend Taylor Swift’s concert in Los Angeles with her friend Lucy Fraser. While the Duchess of Sussex did not accompany her husband on his trip, she took time to enjoy a night out.

If this does not send rumors mills against the Sussexes in full drive, then what will. Reports of a rough patch in Meghan Markle and Prince Harry‘s relationship have been going on for months. Amid speculations about status of the Duke and Duchess of Sussexes’ relationship, many were keen to see if Meghan would follow her husband to Asia on his recent business trip. The Duchess chose not to. However she did take time out to spend a fun evening with her British friend Lucy Fraser.

Meghan Markle attends Taylor Swift’s LA concert

A source told People that Meghan was among the thousands of fans who jammed to Taylor’s songs. She attended the show on Tuesday, August 8, SoFi stadium concert.

Meghan, 42, reportedly jumped out of her chair while singing along to Taylor’s You Belong with Me. Surprisingly the Duchess managed to stay under the radar, despite attending an event that’s heavily covered by media. There is not a single picture of Meghan and her friend Lucy.

From the looks of it Meghan really wanted to keep the evening low profile for herself. While the Duchess was having a ball with her friends, Prince Harry was all smiles as he arrived in Tokyo on August 9.

Meghan skipped Harry’s trip amid rumors of ‘trouble in paradise’

While Harry beamed at the media cameras while arriving at Tokyo on August 9. But his better half Meghan was nowhere in sight.

Harry strutted down at Tokyo’s Haneda Airport in his cap that featured the Archewell Foundation’s logo with a casual black polo shirt. He looked relaxed and was especially smiling while talking to his best bud Nacho Figueras.

While speaking to Mirror about his demeanour, a body language expert said that Harry seemed to enjoying the “celebrity-style attention and approval” he was getting.

Harry had Meghan on his mind during his Japan trip – report says

Harry was apparently shopping for a gift to give his wife after his business in Japan wrapped. After attending the ISPS Sports Values Summit-Special Edition on Wednesday, August 9, the Duke of Sussex hit out local stores with his friend Nacho.

“Shopping for our wives,” Nacho, 46, wrote in his Instagram caption for a picture that featured him and Harry. They were surrounded by backpacks, pens, slippers and more as they modelled a pair of sunglasses.
