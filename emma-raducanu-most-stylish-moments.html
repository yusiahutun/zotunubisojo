Emma Raducanu: the tennis star's most stylish moments from Wimbledon to the Met Gala - see photos

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 8, 2024

Despite not taking part in Wimbledon this year, Emma Raducanu is still at the top of her game when it comes to serving looks. Although the tennis star is still a relative newcomer to the spotlight, the 20-year-old has wasted no time in earning points for some major style moments following her high-profile win at the US Open in 2021.

Emma, soared up the rankings after her historic Grand Slam, becoming British number one, and in July 2022 had a career high of tenth in the world. Currently she is an ambassador for the house of Dior and has modelled pieces designed by the brand's creative director Maria Grazia Chiuri, as well as posed as the face of Dior's skincare and makeup lines.

"Maria Grazia’s work empowers women to feel confident in the iconic Dior cut, which I feel is very unique," Emma told British Vogue last year, "The sincerity of her approach and the way she turns shows into collective and meaningful events unquestionably influenced me in my choice."

With Wimbledon 2023 now upon us Hello! Fashion takes a look at some of Emma's most memorable fashion moments so far.

Emma Raducanu at… the 2021 Met Gala

For her red carpet debut, Emma jumped straight into the deep end by gracing last year's Met Gala in a monochrome Chanel ensemble. The tennis star wore a patterned crop top, midi skirt and matching shawl and finished off her look with a statement pearl chain belt.

Emma Raducanu at… the No Time To Die premiere

Emma's 'Bond Girl' moment was picture perfect – she opted for an asymmetric silver Dior gown with a pleated skirt and rope belt detailing alongside silver jewels by Tiffany & Co.

Emma Raducanu at… British Vogue x Tiffany & Co. dinner

  At a dinner hosted during London Fashion Week in September 2021, Emma wore a halter neck embellished top, white broderie anglaise finely pleated skirt and barely-there silver sandals.

Emma Raducanu at… 'Women Supporting Women'

 The tennis star wore a white one-shoulder mini dress, part of Dior's SS/22 resort collection, featuring a statement classically-inspired motif. Emma paired her dress with jewels by Tiffany & Co. and simple black heeled sandals.

Emma Raducanu… on her first holiday in seven years

At the end of 2021, Emma revealed in an Instagram post that she was taking a holiday for the first time in nearly a decade, telling fans, "Couple flicks from my first holiday in 7 yrs!! Feeling so ready & pumped for pre season." Her well-earned break saw her soaking up the sun in a white short-sleeved button down shirt, lightweight drawstring straight-leg trousers and some pink slides.

Emma Raducanu... in Tiffany & Co. 

 The 19-year-old wonderkid defeated Alison Van Uytvanck in the opening round of Wimbledon on 27 June, wearing £30,000 worth of Tiffany jewellery. Game. Set. Match.

Emma Raducanu... at Kensington Palace

The Dior ambassador attended a lavish dinner at Kensington Palace a dinner to celebratethe opening of 'The Fabulous World of Dior at Harrods'. She put an elegant twist on the cut-out trend in a black floral dress with both shoulder and off the shoulder straps. A simple cross necklace completed her bijouterie.

Emma Raducanu... receiving her MBE

Emma received an MBE in 2022 for her commitment to sport. Her outfit deserved just as much recognition as she wore an ultra chic black midi dress from Dior complete with lace panelling, a pleated midi skirt and an oversized waist belt. The pièce de résistance was her classic black boater hat.

Emma Raducanu... at Annabel's

Emma attended the British Vogue and Tiffany & Co. Fashion And Film Party at private members club Annabel's in February this year. She stunned in a red maxi skirt with a daring lace bodysuit with a black bandeau underneath.

Emma Raducanu... at the Paridas Open

She made the case for Barbiecore on court wearing a bright pink t-shirt with a plum coloured pleated skirt at the BNP Paridas Open in the USA.

Emma Raducanu... at Dior Cruise

Emma jetted off to Mexico for Dior's show in May. She wore a chic black satin skirt with a waistcoat adorned with incredible gold embroidery and held a fan as her coolest accessory. She shared photos to her own Instagram and expressed she, "was in love with this embroidery".
