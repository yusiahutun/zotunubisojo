You Go Girl!Little Dancer Perfectly Channels Aretha Franklin With Sassy Respect Recital Routin

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, February 8, 2024

All this little girl wants is a little R-E-S-P-E-C-T!

In a new viral video — with more than three million views on YouTube! — a six-year-old dance student named Johanna stole the show at her recital by perfectly boogying to Aretha Franklin's hit "Respect."

"She practiced very hard. She would make me play [the song] on Pandora constantly. All eyes were on her from the beginning. As soon as she walked out, everybody was just floored," the girl's mom, Elissa Colón told Today of her daughter's sassy performance.
