Denise Richards' racy OnlyFans where she 'shows boobies' just like topless teen daughter

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, April 29, 2024

Like any mother and daughter, Denise Richards and Sami Sheen want do things together - including stripping off on OnlyFans.

Wild Things star and Bond Girl Denise, who celebrates her 52nd birthday today, joined the subscription site in June 2022, just days after her 18-year-old daughter Sami launched her own racy profile.

Sami sparked a furore when she joined the site and went topless, drawing criticism from people including her dad, controversial actor and recovering sex and drug addict Charlie Sheen.

She announced to her 14 million Instagram followers that she was joining the site. Sami told TMZ she was fine with it, saying: "I don't mind at all, like, I'm happy for her," adding that she was "doing really well" on there.

In her launch post, Denise wrote: “Ready … here we go,” before adding the hashtag “#onlyfans” and directing people to the link to subscribe to her content for $25 a month.

Charlie, 57, had appeared to blame ex-wife Denise for Sami's decision.

He told Page Six: “She is 18 years old now and living with her mother. This did not occur under my roof.

“I do not condone this, but since I’m unable to prevent it, I urged her to keep it classy, creative and not sacrifice her integrity.”

However, Denise hit back saying: “Sami is 18, and this decision wasn’t based on whose house she lives in. All I can do as a parent is guide her and trust her judgment, but she makes her own choices.”

The World is Not Enough star was then hit with further backlash after Sami announced the two of them would be collaborating on the site - a move critics branded "inappropriate and disgusting".

Sami had told her subscribers: "As you know, I'm shooting with my mom soon @deniserichards if you tip in, I'll make sure to send you a [sic] extra special and sweet video from both of us."

Denise responded to criticism, saying: "My daughter got a lot of backlash for opening the account. I had heard of OnlyFans but I wasn't educated on what OnlyFans was."

"Once I started to learn about it, I really think that the creators of the site really took the best of every platform of social media and put it into one site.

"We all post pictures with ourselves with bathing suits on Instagram and some of the other sites that there's no difference other than you actually own the content."

She later explained what she would be flaunting on the site - and why.

She said. "I do bikini. I do lingerie. I do stuff that is sexier because I also think, 'Why not?' If I'm able to do stuff outside of some of the more conservative stuff that's on my Instagram.

"I do show my boobies, my tush. They're already out there if you Google them. Sometimes you have to do something else once in a while."

Reiterating her rationale, she elaborated in an interview on the Jeff Lewis show on Sirius XM radio.

She said: "I've been a bikini model, a lingerie model, and now I do more sexual stuff because I think, 'Why not,' if I'm able to go beyond the more demure content that's on my Instagram.

"I show my breasts and my vagina, and you can find it out there if you Google it. Sometimes you have to go a little bit further, if just one time."

Also on the show, she revealed that her husband, Aaron Phypers, who she married in 2018, shoots "a lot" of her stuff.

She said: "Aaron takes a lot of my content for me.

"He knows what guys like. I ask him, I show him things and say, ‘What do you think?'"

Ever the entrepreneur, the Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills star slashed prices on her explicit account to mark the Black Friday celebrations in November.

And she shows no signs of stopping. In January she offered a free trial for three days

Committing to keeping the profile going, she told SheKnows in December: “I think it’s important as a woman to do what we want to do and not make decisions on being judged on what other people may think or say.”
