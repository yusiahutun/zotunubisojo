Prophetic declarations for breakthroughs and blessings in your life

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, May 20, 2024

A closed mouth is a closed destiny. If you are a Christian, you need to open your mouth wide and declare your breakthrough and liberty daily. As you begin to make prophetic declarations for breakthroughs based on the word on God, you will start to see them manifest in real life.

A decree is a command that something must happen. Decrees and declarations have been used for thousands of years by governments to deliver judgments. Prophetic declarations are God’s system of executing His plans on earth. The apostolic prophetic declarations are based on the word of God. They will help you confront all the challenges you are facing in life.

Prophetic declarations for breakthroughs

There is no breakthrough without prayer. When spoken aloud in agreement with the word of God, declarations will bring breakthroughs. If you believe in the power of prophetic declarations to bring forth breakthroughs, you can shout these words out aloud.

Prophetic declaration for open doors

Doors are important both in the physical and the spiritual realm. When a strongman closes your door and hides the keys from you, it will be hard to make progress. If you believe God for divine open doors, you need to make these declarations based on God's word.

  • I decree that every barrier is broken down, and everything causing blockage along my path is destroyed by the hand of God in the name of Jesus.
  • The Lord God Almighty will open unto me divine doors, doors of breakthrough, doors of greatness, doors of favour, doors of success, doors of promotion, doors of prosperity, in Jesus Name.
  • No door of progress, breakthroughs will ever be shut against me anymore! I will never suffer the frustration of closed doors anymore in Jesus name!
  • I decree that the doors of heavenly treasures are open permanently to me in Jesus name.
  • Let there be a way where there’s no way. Let there be an opened door in impossible places. Let a miracle happen without a stoppage in the name of Jesus Christ.
  • Every door shut against me, causing a delay in my marital and financial breakthrough, is shattered in Jesus name!
  • Holy Ghost, open by thunder, every door shut against fruitfulness in my life in Jesus name.
  • Every door that may have been shut against my destiny, I curse it in Jesus name!
  • Like Daniel, the gates will bow down before me, and the kings of the earth shall knock on my door to ask for my wisdom!
  • Oh Lord, open by fire every door shut against my prosperity or investment, in Jesus name.
  • Angels of the Most High God open every door shut against fruitfulness in my life.
  • This season shall present unto me open doors for great achievements & breakthroughs.
  • I use the key of David to open doors of abundance, prosperity in the name of Jesus.
  • I pronounce that the fountains of heavens be opened upon me and the divine blessings without measure rain on me in the name of Jesus.
  • All doors of goodness that have been closed against me, I command them today to open in Jesus name.
  • I decree God is going before me, making crooked places straight. He has already lined up the right people, the right opportunities and solutions to problems I haven’t had. What he promised will come to pass.
