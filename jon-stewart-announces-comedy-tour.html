Jon Stewart Announces Comedy Tour

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, January 1, 0001

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — Fresh from his return to Comedy Central’s “The Daily Show” the noted stand-up comedian and political commentator Jon Stewart announced a series of live stand-up performances on the East Coast.

Produced by Live Nation, the five-date comedy tour kicks off March 1st at the Bardavon 1869 Opera House in Poughkeepsie, NY with additional shows in New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut, before closing out at the Grand Opera House in Delaware on April 17th.

The event will be phone-free, with the use of phones, smart watches, and accessories prohibited inside of the venue during the performance.

Fans will be asked to check their phones in individual Yondr pouches that can be opened at the conclusion of the show. Guests maintain possession of their devices at all times and can access them throughout the event only in designated Phone Use Areas within the venue. All devices will be re-secured in Yondr pouches before returning to the performance space.

Tickets for the show went on sale on Tuesday and are available at https://events.seated.com/jon-stewart

AN EVENING WITH JON STEWART TOUR DATES:
Fri Mar 01 – Poughkeepsie, NY – Bardavon 1869 Opera House
Fri Mar 15 – York, PA – Appell Center for the Performing Arts
Fri Mar 29 – Princeton, NJ – McCarter Theatre Center
Wed Apr 10 – Stamford, CT – The Palace Theatre
Sat Apr 27 – Wilmington, DE – The Grand Opera House
