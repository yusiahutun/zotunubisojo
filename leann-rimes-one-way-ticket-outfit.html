LeAnn Rimes Shuts Down the Stage in a Barely-There Dress During Emotional Performance

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, March 17, 2024

If there was ever a doubt that LeAnn Rimes is an incredible performer, we ask you to look at one of her latest Instagram videos.

In mid-February, the "god's work" singer posted a clip of her performance at Gruene Hall in Texas. In it, she's wearing a stunning low-cut orange dress that has a thigh-high slit (you can shop something similar on Amazon). She topped off her look with a felt cowboy hat, which seemed most appropriate for the venue.

And while there are many comments about how beautiful LeAnn looked, her vocals, as usual, were what most people were talking about.

LeAnn belted out her 1996 hit "One Way Ticket," for the crowd, but instead of its original pop country vibe, LeAnn went with a more soulful rendition that absolutely blew us away.

And we weren't the only ones who were impressed. "One of the best country songs of all time," one Instagram comment read. "Leeann freakin rimes," said another. While there were also many heart emojis sprinkled through the comments section.

Trending Now

LeAnn has been doing intimate shows for a while now as she continues to promote her latest album, "god's work." In an interview with Country Living last year, the singer opened up about how personal the creation of the album was for her. "As I explored all of these different facets of myself, I got to ask some pretty deep questions about my own upbringing, you know, the way we've been programmed as a society. I think really this album is full of unanswered questions."
