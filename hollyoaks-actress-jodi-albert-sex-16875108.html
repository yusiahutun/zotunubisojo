Hollyoaks babe Jodi Albert reclaims sex symbol status in sheer lingerie expos

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Our Instagram Babe of the Week title goes to Jodi Albert, 34.

The Brit actress rose to fame in 2002, when she joined Channel 4 soap Hollyoaks as Debbie Dean.

An instant hit among fans, the brunette bombshell soon found herself listed on the annual FHM 100 Sexiest Women list.

As viewers of the show will know all too well, the actress was part of the early 00s Hollyoaks bombshells set.

Jodi joined the likes of actresses Sarah-Jayne Dunn, 36 (Mandy Richardson), Gemma Atkinson, 33 (Lisa Hunter), Carley Stenson, 35 (Steph Cunningham) and Ali Bastian, 36 (Becca Dean).

The group famously took part in scantily-clad photoshoots during their time on the show.

After leaving the soap in 2005, Jodi married her long-term love, Westlife singer Kian Egan, 38, and the pair now have three children.

While Albert has been largely absent from the small screen in recent years, she's still very much aware of her bombshell status.

Taking to her Jodi Albert Instagram recently, the actress posted a jaw-dropping picture of herself modelling lacy black lingerie.

Amping up the raunch factor in a sheer-panelled bodysuit, the mum-of-three looked in her element as she offered a smouldering glare to the camera.

In response to the snap, one admirer wrote: "You look absolutely stunning."

While another said: "Unreal, Jodi. You're just class," alongside three heart-eyed emojis and a kissing face.

Keen to show his approval, proud husband Kian commented: "My sexy hot wife," followed by a red heart.

Responding to the compliment, a fan told the singer: "You're a lucky man."

Instagram

In agreement, an Instagram user told Albert: "You've always been the sexiest mum in the world."

Jodi delighted fans last weekend, when she shared a picture of herself enjoying a catch-up lunch with fellow Hollyoaks babes Sarah-Jayne, Carley and Ali.

Praising the snap, one soap fan wrote: "Seeing all you girls together and wishing you would go back to Hollyoaks."

Another quipped: "Looks like a scene from Sex and the City," alongside a kissing face.
