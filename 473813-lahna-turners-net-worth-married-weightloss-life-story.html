Comedy

After graduating from college, she began her career in the comedy sector by performing three comedic songs at a local open mic night. Two months later, she booked her first paying gig. She released her first comedy album, Di*k Jokes and Other Assorted Love Songs, in 2004.

Lahna recorded Is These Lips Could Talk in 2012. This was followed by her first one-hour special titled So…I Wrote a Song About It in 2014 and Limeade in 2017. Limeade was Turner’s first ever comedy visual album.

Comedian Lahna Turner's albums have received massive airplay at top radio stations across the country. They have also been played in National Lampoon's Top 40 comedy countdown and are in rotation on SiriusXM Satellite Radio. In addition, Lahna has an early, non-comedy album titled Life as a Human.
