The Scotsman Food Truck Park Laurel Mercantile

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, February 19, 2024

Visitors and locals alike enjoy our newest outdoor eating experience at the Scotsman Food Truck Park. Located right beside the Scotsman General Store, you'll find a diverse variety of the local culinary flair - something for the whole family to enjoy! 

Good luck trying to choose from these options! 

1. Papa Dough's Pizza, with a menu of over ten different handmade pizzas and flavors

2. JDQ BBQ, with a menu full of award winning brisket and pulled pork

While you're waiting on your food, don't forget to grab a vintage glass bottle soda, your favorite snack or one of our New Orleans-style Scotsman Snowballs inside the General Store!

Play a few outdoor games on the turf and be sure to check out our observation deck for a photo with our newest mural. It's a fun place to sit and eat, too!
