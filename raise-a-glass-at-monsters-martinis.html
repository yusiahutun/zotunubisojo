Raise a Glass at Monsters &amp; Martinis

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 24, 2024

The Humane Society of the Treasure Coast will partner with Barkin’ for a Park to host Monsters & Martinis, a Halloween-themed Homecoming event, October 28. The scary affair will take over the Rockin H Ranch Events Venue in Palm City from 6 to 10 p.m.

Guests will enjoy live DJ, light bites, raffles, and games, as well as the crowning of a Homecoming King and Queen. There also will be a costume contest at 8 p.m. Tickets are $75 and can be purchased in advance here.

Participants can create a personalized Humane Society of the Treasure Coast Homecoming fundraising page to share with family and friends. All funds raised from Monsters & Martinis will directly benefit the animals in the shelter’s care. A fundraising page may be created here.

The top-fundraising man and woman will be crowned King and Queen and will enjoy all the perks of their newfound royalty, including: Two tickets to the August 2023 Pup Crawl; two tickets to the October 2023 Humane Society of the Treasure Coast Homecoming; a choice in naming rights for one animal up for adoption at the shelter; a one quarter-page ad in the Clawsmopawlitan, the keepsake program at the Paws & Claws Gala 2023; and one ticket to the Paws & Claws Gala 2023 (with a minimum of $1,000 raised).

For more information, contact Community Events Specialist Debbi Greer at (772) 600-3214 or DGreer@hstc1.org.
