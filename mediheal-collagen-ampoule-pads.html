Mediheals Collagen Ampoule Pads Clean Pores &amp; Boost Elasticity

If you often look to TikTok for the next addition to your beauty regimen, then you’ve likely already heard of the brand Mediheal. Its skin-tightening chin mask sold out a record three times, and even BTS member Jimin swears by the $2 Tea Tree Care Solution Essential Mask. But if you’ve already given both of those best-sellers a go, it’s time to check out Mediheal’s Collagen Ampoule Pad , a collagen-infused toner pad that firms skin and restores elasticity. 

Environmental aggressors are a leading cause of skin concerns, so if you’ve dealt with your fair share of congestion, redness, dryness or premature aging, these toner pads are for you. The daily skincare pads offer a quick solution to “improve skin depression” across multiple areas of the face, such as the eye area, cheeks, smile lines and forehead.

Each disposable pad is made with vegan fibers and packed full of three key ingredients that nourish acne-prone, mature and sensitive skin—collagen, ceramides and milk protein sink deep into the complexion to fix sagging, grime build-up and wrinkles. 

Amazon reviewers have flocked to the reviews section to share their experiences using the collagen pads. Many have commented on how helpful the inclusion of tweezers is for keeping the container sanitary, while others mostly remarked on how “amazing” their skin feels after each use.

“I’ve seen these toner pads advertised all over social media and I can see why! They’re honestly amazing. I have sensitive and acne prone skin so it’s difficult to find a toner that won’t eat at my skin. This is so easy to use, so gentle on my skin, and it smells very nice…It’s been about a week of use for me and I already know I’m going to stock up on more. I also love how much of the product you get for the price,” wrote one.

“In a matter of ONE day this thing got rid of almost all of the clogged pores on my inner cheeks (around my nose, right under my eyes),” shared another. “I had these, what looked like, little hills across my cheeks and nothing could get rid of them. I bought these on a whim per TikTok, and left the patch on much like a mask for a little over 15 mins. Then I used what was left and rubbed it on my entire face…By morning I had very very little clogged pores left. My make up went on smooth! This is a game changer!”

In addition to the Collagen Ampoule Pads, Mediheal offers a variety of other toner formulations for targeting different concerns. Pick up a container of gentle toner pads for blemished skin or opt for these brightening pads to lighten dark spots and dull areas.
