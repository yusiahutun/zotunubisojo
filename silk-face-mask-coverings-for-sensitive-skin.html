14 best silk face masks for people with sensitive skin or fighting maskne

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 9, 2024

If you're worried about maskne, a silk face covering might be your best option

    Even though lockdown is easing, wearing a face covering over our nose and mouth is still essential when using public transport and entering shops. 

    There are funny masks to show your sense of humour, designer masks and stylish masks that you can find at some of your favourite high street shops - but what about those of us that are struggling with maskne? (The acne caused by wearing face coverings). With maskne on the rise, we've found that silk face coverings are your best option when it comes to sensitive skin.

    RELATED: Best face masks made by your favourite lingerie brands

    Will a silk face covering prevent maskne?

    Experts also say that face coverings made of silk are the best to wear if you have problem skin or are worried about the dreaded maskne. Silk face masks are also gentler on your skin compared with polyester or other types of face masks, and are more breathable than multi-layer cotton. Then there's the touchability factor – silk masks are decidedly more luxurious.
