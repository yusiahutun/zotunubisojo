Biography Timeline

  • 1964: Stella graduates from Sevier County High School in Sevierville, Tennessee.
  • 1967: She moves to Nashville, Tennessee, to pursue a music career.
  • 1970: Stella signs with Elektra Records and releases her debut album, “In the Garden,” which features her first charting single, “I’ve Loved You All Over the World.”
