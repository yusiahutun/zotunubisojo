TinaKitten is a South Korean Twitch streamer, content maker, gamer, and internet personality. TinaKitten rocketed to fame thanks to her Twitch channel, ‘@TinaKitten.’

TinaKitten’s Net Worth

Twitch and sponsorships are two of this gamer’s primary money streams. Tina’s net worth is estimated to be around $300,000 as of November 2022.

Biography of TinaKitten

TinaKitten made her debut on June 30, 1998. Her full name is Christina “Tina” Kenyon. Her zodiac sign is Cancer, and she is 24 years old. She was born in South Korea but later moved to Los Angeles, California, in the United States.

As a result, she is half Korean and half white. This public figure has never acknowledged her parents on social media. Tina, on the other hand, is related to someone.

Career

TinaKitten is well-known for her outstanding gaming and artistic skills. She gained to notoriety after a viral Among Us game in which she failed to answer a simple Simon Says puzzle four times in a row. Furthermore, her most famous quote throughout her gaming streams is squealing, “Please!”

This gamer frequently streams with her buddies, where they play games like Karl, Among Us, Stardew Valley, Minecraft, Raft, Foolish, Corpse, Sykkuno, and many others.
