And after he left prison, he concentrated on his music, getting many fans in his country. But unfortunately, the style of music glorifies the drug trade. Because of the lyrics in the song, the genre is primarily prohibited, and those that pursue this lifestyle are often in danger.

Who is Marisela Vallejos Felix?

Marisela Vallejos Felix is famous for being the late musician and songwriter's wife. She was born in the 1970s in Mexicali, Northwest Mexico. This means that she is in her fifties in 2022. Her parents are Ramona Hernandez and Gabriel Sanchez.

She did not live in Mexico and migrated to the United States of America, where she spent most of her life. And after living in the country for 30 years, Marisela Vallejos Felix got American citizenship.

Aside from her formative years, not much is known about her adult life aside from her relationship with the famous Mexican musician.

Is Marisela Vallejos Felix still alive?

Marisela is still alive and is a United States of America citizen. She got her citizenship in 2018 and described the process as involving. According to her, she had to prove her source of income as she is the widow of the King of Corrido.
