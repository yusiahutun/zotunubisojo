Kandyse McClure Biography, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Candice McClure is a South African-born Canadian Actress. Her real name is Candice Kandyse McClure. The beautiful talented actress is known for her role in many popular films and TV series such as Hemlock grave, Ghost war, Da Vinci Inquest, V wars, The client list, and many more. Her work is appreciated a lot by her audience.

Early Life

McClure belongs to the city of Durban, South Africa, and was born on 22nd March 1980. She has spent a lot of time with her grandparents as well. Her father’s name was Marcel while there are no reports available regarding her mother. Although she did mention Lizanne as her mother quite a few times, especially on the occasion of mother’s day. She likes to keep her life private and as of now, has not shared her family details in the mainstream media yet. She did her schooling at West Vancouver Secondary School in 1998 and she remained an intelligent student all through her studies.

Kandyse McClure Career

Kandsye acting career took off in the year 1999 where she appeared as a lead actor in the film named “In the class of his own”. This was her first show from where she got the motivation of considering Acting as a profession. In the year 2000 she received small roles to work in the series or films such as “Romeo must die” directed by Andrzej Bartkowiak followed by the Teen drama “Higher Ground” and “Just deal”. Acting in these films and series her performance was appreciated by the public and was recognized, this was also a sign of her bright future in this profession.

In the year 2002, her work was notable in Stephen King’s Carrie where her character was named Susan D. “Sue Snell”. She spent 21 years in her acting career where she appeared in a total of 11 movies and 48 TV series. Her work includes acting, dubbing, and some of the credits in recurring roles. Due to her fabulous work, she also got an opportunity to work with famous Hollywood celebrities as well.

Awards and Nominations

In the year 2020, she was nominated for Best Supporting Actress in the Leo Awards. She later won Peabody Awards for Excellence in Television in the year 2005 for TV series named “Battlestar Galactica”. Kandyse works with a nonprofit organization named CARE Canada to take care of the poverty situation in developing countries. Since she is Cape Coloured, her ancestor is all about helping the living being in every possible way. She also came and joined hands to support equal rights for black people as she stands against discrimination.
