Katie Price treats herself to beauty salon trip as she prepares to jet off on holiday to Turkey

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

KATIE Price treated herself to a beauty salon trip as she prepares to jet off on holiday to Turkey.

The former glamour model, 42, was spotted leaving The LA Studio in Shoreham, Sussex, while holding a coffee cup.

Dressed in a pink tracksuit combo and sporting a backpack, Katie looked like a new woman after her beauty treatment.

The star smiled and joked with the staff, a week after getting extensions put into her hair at the same salon.

The mum-of-five got the makeover ahead of her trip to Turkey - where she was left "heartbroken" after Harvey was deemed unfit to go on holiday with her.

She planned on taking the 18-year-old with her and new boyfriend Carl Woods, 31, as well as kids Junior, 15, and Princess 13.

But doctor's have ruled Harvey unfit to travel after he was released from hospital earlier this week after 10 days - including time spent in intensive care.

A source told The Sun Online: "Doctors told Katie it would be better for Harvey's health if he stayed at home. She's heartbroken but of course she'll take doctors advice.

"Harvey will stay at home with his carer and Katie will still go - she's taking Princess and Junior and some of their friends and she didn't want to cancel their holiday and let them down, especially as Princess's birthday party was disrupted by Harvey going to hospital. "

A representative for Katie confirmed her devastation and said she had decided to go ahead for the benefit of her other kids.

"Katie has had to make the heartbreaking decision to keep Harvey at home after doctors advised he should remain in the UK after his most recent hospital stay," said the spokesman.

The Sun Online revealed earlier this week that Harvey had returned home after getting the all clear from doctors, with Katie beaming: "It feels like Christmas. I can't stop smiling.

"The day started badly but it's ending as the best day ever."

The mother-of-five was left devastated when her eldest child was rushed to hospital after he suffered breathing problems and his temperature shot to 42C.
