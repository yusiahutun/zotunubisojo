Meghan Markle's affordable Le Specs sunglasses are back in stock but probably not for long

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, April 25, 2024

Style alert! Snap up a pair of Duchess Meghan's 'Air Hearts' cat-eye sunglasses by Le Specs

    Meghan Markle has the coolest collection of sunglasses, and when she stepped out for her New York City baby shower back while pregnant with son Archie, one thing we noticed is she looked as glam as ever with her movie star shades.

    The Duchess of Sussex's oversized sunglasses – the Air Heart style by Le Specs – keep selling out, but the good news is the sunnies, which are currently retailing for around a £55 ($60) retail price, are available again.

    The acetate and gold-tone sunnies are in a timeless cat-eye silhouette that’s flattering for all face shapes. 

    Le Specs 'Air Heart' sunglasses, £55 / $60 at Net-a-Porter

    And as someone who owns a couple of pairs of Le Specs styles, I can see why Meghan loves them (and even Jennifer Lopez has a pair). They're so reasonably priced but look expensive, even up close!

    Meghan Markle's Le Specs sunglasses collection

    The Duchess of Sussex is such a fan of Le Specs that she actually has three pairs: the 'Air Heart's, as well as the 'Bandwagon' and 'Le Danzing' – the latter of which she wore in September 2023 to the Invictus Games in Germany.

    When Duchess Meghan has worn Le Specs 'Air Heart' sunglasses

    Meghan was spotted wearing Le Specs 'Air Heart' sunglasses in February 2019 as she left Manhattan's The Mark Hotel during her glamorous baby shower weekend, teaming them with winter-ready black skinny maternity jeans by Hatch and a William Vintage Courrèges coat.

    The royal was also spotted rocking the very same shades in December 2021, during a shopping trip in Montecito, California. Meghan teamed her sunnies with a smart brown wool coat by Massimo Dutti.

    The Air Hearts, with their retro Hollywood vibe, have a cool celebrity following, with Olivia Palermo also a fan of the style.

    These classic shades will look great with just about any outfit from blue jeans to a little black dress, or even nothing but a swimsuit!
