Good Samaritan tries to rescue 'injured baby owl' before she realises it's actually a MUSHROOM

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, January 31, 2024

When Rachael Lee saw what appeared to be an injured baby owl in her mother’s garden, she felt compelled to investigate further.

The bird seemed to be stuck still and had one of its eyes closed, so postal worker Miss Lee decided to rescue it.
