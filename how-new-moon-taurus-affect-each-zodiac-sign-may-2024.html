New Moon in Taurus Will Affect Each Zodiac Sign 2024

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, October 7, 2024

You might be curious to see how the new moon in Taurus 2024 will affect each zodiac sign since this represents a chance for new beginnings. Luckily, this fixed earth sign can steady whatever course of action you may want to focus on. You can create a luxurious life if you invoke the tenacity and determination of Taurus during this lunar cycle.

Typically, Taurus season is rather easygoing. The leisurely pace is something we can all look forward to since no other zodiacal season is this calm or comforting. However, Taurus season 2024 is unlike any other. Just a few weeks earlier, Jupiter in Taurus joined Uranus in Taurus on April 20 for the first time in fourteen years.

When Jupiter and Uranus collide, an unexpected twist in luck may occur. Jupiter typically represents growth, abundance, and expansion whereas Uranus denotes change, unpredictability, and plot twists. When these two work together, there might be larger-than-life twists and turns. Since both planets are in Taurus, you may notice extreme differences in your financial state, material items, and value systems. You may find that you desire radically different things in your life as this rare transit unfolds. Where could your journey lead?

New pathways will emerge with the new moon in Taurus on May 7, 2024, at 11:21 p.m. ET, which will occur 18 degrees. New moons represent a new chapter in your life. The following six months could be an exceptionally productive period as your manifestations come to fruition. So, the new moon in Taurus will be an excellent time to set your intentions! Plus, this new moon is special. It’s the first new moon in Taurus that hasn’t doubled as an eclipse in the last year. And the moon is exalted in Taurus, so it’s an especially strong lunation.

A feeling of stability will settle in during the lunation. You might feel more secure with what you value and desire. Your steadiness can help you lay the groundwork for the lunar cycle. Given that this lunation is occurring in an earth sign, you should do something tangible to kick off your manifestations. Luna will also answer to Venus—the planetary ruler of Taurus—so you could create something to represent what you want to manifest. Art, music, or another form of craftsmanship could help you set your intentions in stone. 

As expected, the new moon will be in perfect alignment with the sun in Taurus. The sun-moon alignment is also part of a greater Taurus stellium, which includes Venus, Jupiter, and Uranus. Since the Taurus stellium will be immensely strong, you will be called to consider the physical means in your world. The Taurus stellium will encourage you to pursue whatever aligns with your need for comfort. Items like clothes, or services that enhance your self-care may become more important to you during this time. It’s not to say that you’ll become materialistic or greedy. But it’s to say that your personal belongings and expenses are important for your overall quality of life. Move with grace, humility, and tenacity throughout the six-month lunar cycle as you build a luxurious life. 

Aside from the Taurus stellium, the new moon in Taurus will also harmonize with Saturn in Pisces. Taskmaster Saturn will encourage you to be responsible for fulfilling your lunar manifestations and intentions. The following six months could be incredibly productive if you put in the work. You may feel like your wildest dreams are plausible. As long as you are realistic, consistent, and hopeful, you can accomplish anything you desire during this lunar cycle.
