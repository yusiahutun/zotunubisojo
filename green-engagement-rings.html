The most beautiful green engagement rings

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 29, 2024

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Tiffany & Co

Tiffany & Co

Tiffany & Co's timeless 'Novo' ring is designed with a round emerald centre, that sits on a sleek band of micro-pavé-set diamonds for added sparkle.

Rachel Boston

Rachel Boston

Rachel Boston's bold aesthetic challenges the traditional world of bridal jewellery, by working with all kinds of diamonds from rough, imperfect stones to refined white diamonds. This piece is one of a kind.

Minka Jewels

Minka Jewels

Minka Jewels' green stone ring is from the brand's Athena collection, which takes inspiration from classical Greek architecture.

Emma Clarkson Webb

Emma Clarkson Webb

Emma Clarkson Webb sources each diamond specifically to fit her clients' requests. This 18ct gold single-stone ring demonstrates the intricacy and dedication of her work, as the stone took months to find.

Octavia Elizabeth

Octavia Elizabeth

Octavia Elizabeth's design features a horizontal Tourmaline stone and a recycled handmade golden band. We can see from this beautiful ring why celebrities such as Gigi Hadid and Jessica Alba are firm favourites of the designer.

Shay

Shay's delicate design features three beautiful teardrop emeralds, that together form a heart shape – we guarantee it will look elegant resting on the wearer's finger.
