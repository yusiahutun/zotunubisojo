1. Size of the Office Space

The size of the office space is a crucial factor that affects the price of office cleaning services in Malaysia. Here are some steps to consider when assessing the size of your office space:

  • Measure the total square footage of the office, including individual rooms and common areas.
  • Identify any specialized areas that require additional attention, such as conference rooms or kitchen areas.
  • Consider the layout and configuration of the office space. A complex layout with multiple levels or hard-to-reach areas may require more time and effort to clean.
  • Determine the number of workstations or desks that need regular cleaning.
  • Factor in the number and type of restrooms in the office, as they require specific cleaning and maintenance.

    • By following these steps, you can accurately assess the size of your office space and understand how it impacts the pricing of cleaning services.
