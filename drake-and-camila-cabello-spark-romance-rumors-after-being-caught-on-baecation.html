Drake and Camila Cabello spark romance rumors after being caught on 'baecation'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

There may be a new couple in town, as Drake and Camila Cabello were seen hanging out and riding jet skis together in the rapper’s fave holiday destination, Turks and Caicos.

The could-be celebrity couple were spotted having an ‘intimate’ conversation, and riding off into the waves together. Of course, this doesn’t mean they’re dating. However, as far as public knowledge knows, both parties are single, and the internet loves to speculate. Luckily, some eagle-eyed Drake fans in the same location captured videos for the rest of us to analyze, and social media is going wild.

Drake and Camilla Cabello spotted ‘hanging out’ in Turks and Caicos

The music stars were recently spotted at the lavish destination, jet skiing and having an intimate conversation together, at Noahs Ark Beach Club.

It’s the first time the two have been spotted out in public together. However, it was reported that the Marvin’s Room singer followed the Fifth Harmony alum earlier this year, after she posted lyrics from his song, Search and Rescue in one of her Instagram captions.

Camila, 26, was famously dating Canadian singer Shawn Mendes, however, they ended their ‘on-off relationship’ in June, after two years together.

Drake, real name Aubrey Drake Graham, is also an eligible bachelor and recently revealed he didn’t see himself marrying a celebrity. Although that may be bad news for Camila, it’s great news for fans.

The video is doing the rounds on X, formerly known as Twitter, and of course, fans took to the comments to discuss.

“Camila Cabello and Drake hanging out on a random Wednesday in the Bahamas was NOT on my bingo card of ‘23,” wrote one.

Another said: “The way they are talking clearly shows that they are friends. How tf people got the idea that they are dating.”

“Shawn currently found crying and throwing up,” joked another.

Well, whatever the situation, we’re hoping for a collab sometime soon.

The rapper is taking a ‘break from music’ to focus on his health

The much-deserved vacation comes after Drake announced he’d be taking a break from music to focus on his health.

Just hours after the release of his most recent studio album, For All The Dogs, in October, the 37-year-old announced he wouldn’t be making any more music for a while.

Speaking on his radio show, he said: “I probably won’t make music for a little bit, I’m gonna be honest. “I need to focus on my health, first and foremost — and I’ll talk about that soon enough.”

He revealed his health issues were “nothing crazy” but he’d been dealing with stomach problems for years.

However, it’s not stopping him touring, as just after wrapping up mammoth US Tour alongside 21 Savage, he’s heading back on the road with J Cole in January.
