Who is Mavado:

Mavado is a famous Reggae Singer. He was born on November 30, 1981 and his birthplace is Kingston, Jamaica. Mavado is also well known as, Born David Constantine Brooks, he rose to fame for his singles, “Dying” and “Weh Dem A Do,” which aired on radio stations across the globe. In 2008 he received the UK MOBO Award for Best Reggae Act.

Mavado is originated from Jamaica. He was featured on Drake’s 2010 song “Find Your Love.”
