How To Wear... Leather Culottes

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, March 18, 2024

Media Platforms Design Team

Culottes and leather combined could scare even the most intrepid trendophiles. But the reality is they can be super flattering—and versatile. The culotte shape has the ease of skirting and the hide fabric gives the "pants" a nice volume for playing with balance. The trick is in the outfit-making. Keep your up-top tailored and cropped (or tucked in) and let the culottes do the style-making. For accessories, be as forward as your chosen pants—statement bags and major platforms should do the trick. And when in doubt add a waist-cinching belt.
