Founding and Early Years

Aldershot Town FC was founded in 1992 and emerged from the ashes of the now-defunct Aldershot Football Club. The club has since enjoyed success in various non-league divisions, gradually climbing up the English football pyramid.

Aldershot Town FC plays its home matches at the EBB Stadium, located in Aldershot, Hampshire. The stadium has a seating capacity of 7,100 and provides a fantastic atmosphere for both home and away fans.
