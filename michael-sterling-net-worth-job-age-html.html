Michael Sterling is an established attorney at the law based firm named Michael T. Sterling. Prior to running his current firm, he held the position of Senior Advisor to Mayor of Atlanta. 

He worked as the Assistant United States Attorney in Chicago, Illinois, from 2009 to 2011. 

Michael Sterling's Wife, Wedding

Michael is married to actress and producer Eva Marcille. The two first met in Atlanta, Georgia, and began dating in 2017.
