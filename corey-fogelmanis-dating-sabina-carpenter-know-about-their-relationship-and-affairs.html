Is Corey Fogelmanis Still Dating Sabrina Carpenter Or Dating A Mystery Female. Find Out His Current Relationship Status

The innocent and cute Corey Fogelmanis we all know is now a grown up boy. In today's world having affairs with multiple women certainly, helps in increasing your stature as a man.

Corey's rumored relationship with Sabrina Carpenter in 2015 was one of the most talked about teen relationship at that time. But is the couple still together or Corey's been dating someone new?

Corey Fogelmanis and Sabrina Carpenter-Still Together

If you are one of those dedicated Disney fans, then surely you shipped Cobrina, a.k.a Corey Fogelmanis and Sabrina Carpenter.

Corey with his rumored girlfriend Sabrina Carpenter, Source: M-Magazine

After meeting for the first time during their role in the TV series Girl Meets World, Corey officially confessed his feelings to Sabrina via Instagram post in 2015

In the video, he said, "This morning I realized that I love you."

But to their fan's dismay, the video was only supposed to be a funny bonding time between the co-stars. They never revealed anything about their relationship to the public, which leads to the speculation that they weren't dating at all.

According to the rumors, they started dating in 2015 and quickly became one of the most shipped couples out there. The love birds still remain in good harmony with one another and good friends.

Sabrina and Corey enjoying together, Source: M-Magazine

But their unfortunate fans are still praying that the couple somehow should get together.

Corey Fogelmanis Affairs and Relationship 

Well, apart from his so-called rumored relationship with Sabrina, Corey also dated two of his other co-stars from the same TV series Girls Meets World.

Corey during a photoshoot for a magazine cover, Source: Pinterest

Corey's dating history is quite confusing, to say the least. During his so-called relationship with Sabrina, he was in a relationship with Rowan Blanchard.

Corey in a playful spirit with Rowan Blanchard 

They were pretty close to each other in the early 2015 and by close we mean, too close to comfort. Looks like it was nothing more than a casual hook-up.

Earlier this year, he was also associated with Peyton List. But as all of his previous relationships, this rumor became a deflated balloon as well.

And currently, he's linked with Disney actress Ceci Balagot. They recently uploaded a cute picture of themselves.

Even though she captioned the image by calling him her fake boyfriend, but both of them were looking comfortable together. Maybe they're dating secretly.

Know More About Corey Fogelmanis

Corey Fogelmanis was born on August 13, 1999, in Thousand Oaks, California, the United States to Shannon Fogelmanis and Dain Fogelmanis.

Corey started his career at a tender age of 6

From an early age, he became obsessed with acting. He started his career at the age of 6. Currently, the 17-years-old actor has done over 18 productions across the country.

His notable works include The Pasadena Playhouse in Southern California, and Girls Meets World. He's currently working on his new movie Mostly Ghostly 3: One Night in Doom House.

This teenage actor's net worth is 2 million dollars, as of 2016.
