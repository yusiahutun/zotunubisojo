Lisa Bonet Always Sensed a 'Sinister, Shadow Energy' Around Bill Cosby

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 26, 2024

Lisa Bonet may not have necessarily known what Bill Cosby was doing behind closed doors when she played his daughter on "The Cosby Show," but she did know there was an off-putting energy about him she could never quite put her finger on.

Bonet, who played Denise Huxtable on "The Cosby Show" from 1984-1991, told PorterEdit she sensed an unavoidable "sinister, shadow energy" during her time on the NBC sitcom.
