Ines de Ramon first made the news as Paul Wesley’s girlfriend. Sadly, the duo broke up a few years after getting married in 2019. And now (as of 2022),  the jewelry expert is back in the spotlight romantically paired with the American actor Brad Pitt!

Get to know all about Ines de Ramon including her parents, height, and net worth as this article proceeds.

Meet Ines de Ramon, Brad Pitt Rumored Girlfriend

On November 13m 2022, Ines sparked rumors of a romance with Brad Pitt after the two were spotted appearing cuddly and comfortable together at a Bono concert in Los Angeles.

As seen in the pictures, Ines was comfortable in the arms of the Fight Club actor. Infact, Brad even pulled the beauty close as the two chatted outside the Orpheum Theatre with A-list friends, Cindy Crawford, Rande Gerber, Sean Penn, and Vivi Nevo.

For the concert, Brad dressed casually in jeans, a gray cardigan, and a tan hat, while Ines wore black leather pants and a cropped white t-shirt paired with a bright yellow Fendi Peekaboo bag.

An insider claimed the two “met through a mutual friend” and that the actor is “really into” de Ramon. However, a second source confirmed the pair only “recently started dating,” but were in a non-exclusive relationship. The source also added, “Ines is cute, fun, and energetic. She has a great personality. Brad enjoys spending time with her.”

The news about Ines and Brad comes just a month after he was linked to model Emily Ratajkowski.

Prior to that, Brad dated model Nicole Poturalski in the summer of 2020, and MIT professor and architect Neri Oxman in April 2018.

And not to mention, the actor was married to Angelina Jolie from August 2014 to April 2019. The two also share six children named Maddox, Pax, Zahara, Shiloh, Knox, and Vivienne.

Also, back from July 2000 to Jan 2005, Brad was married to Jennifer Aniston.

Paul Wesley And Ines de Ramon Relationship

Ines and Paul Wesley were first spotted holding hands after a dinner date in June 2018, and it was then, fans knew the two were an item. Fast-forward to February 2019, the duo was again spotted with matching engagement rings, and a few months later, E! News confirmed that the couple did, in fact, get married!

Also, on July 23, 2020, Ines posted a throwback photo from their wedding on IG to celebrate their anniversary.  It seems Ines wore a white wedding dress while the ceremony was held on a beach.

Sadly, the two divorced before they could celebrate their fourth wedding anniversary.

In case you don’t know, Ines wasn’t Paul’s first wife. The actor was previously married to Torrey DeVitto from 2011 to 2013. The pair even costarred in Killer Movie together.
