10 Celebrity Influencers Worth Following

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

When it is already difficult to separate the concepts of influencer and celebrity, now we have a relatively new group, celebrity influencers. Celebrity influencers are people who have gained their reputation through traditional media channels. And subsequently influenced their followers by actively participating in social media platforms in the areas they want to make a difference.

Let’s see the 10 most popular celebrity influencers you should follow as soon as possible.

Kylie Jenner

Although Kylie Jenner is known for her influencer and business ventures, one of the biggest factors in her fame was the reality show she was in with her family. Kylie Jenner shares videos on various topics on her YouTube channel but her most loved ones are definitely the videos she has with her daughter, Stormi. She is also actively using her social media platforms to promote brands. Even though she lands in hot water sometimes.

Hailey Bieber

Hailey Bieber started her career as a model. Firstly, she did photoshoots for prominent fashion magazines and then successfully continued her modeling career by walking on the runways of famous brands. Still modeling, Hailey Bieber opened her own YouTube channel just a few weeks ago. She announced that she will be giving fashion and beauty tips on her channel. One of her first YouTube videos which is with her best friend Kendall Jenner, even became viral as soon as it launched.

Shay Mitchell

Most of us know Shay Mitchell from the hugely popular teen drama Pretty Little Liars. She opened her YouTube channel a few years ago. The Actor usually posts on motherhood, pregnancy and beauty on his YouTube account. She even has a reality series in collaboration with YouTube.

Brie Larson

Oscar-winning actor Brie Larson is also one of the actors who started his YouTube career almost a year ago. Brie Larson usually publishes workout videos and podcasts called “Learning Lots” on her account. Captain Marvel influencing people to work out? Count us in.

Zac Efron

The Disney star we all know from High School Musical, Zack Efron continues his acting career with movies in different genres. At the same time, he has a YouTube Channel where he often shares gym and sports-related videos.

Madelaine Petsch

Madelaine Petsch, who became famous with her role in Riverdale’s Cheryl Blossom, has also been sharing sections of her life on her YouTube channel for a while. Madelaine Petsch said that her purpose for launching a YouTube channel was for fans to see her real life outside of acting. Especially her meditation and workout videos are highly appreciated by her fans. She is also creating collections with fashion brands, and actively using her channels to promote products.

Dwayne Johnson

For people who enjoys insane workouts, Dwayne Johnson is one of the most influential and inspirational celebrity influencers on social media. The Rock uses his Instagram and YouTube account to share inspirational recipe and workout videos. He is the 3rd most followed person with 231M followers on Instagram. He combines his content with humor which attract the attention of many people.

Chrissy Teigen

Model Chrissy Teigen is a celebrity influencer you can see on every social media platform. On her Instagram account, she usually gives recipes, lifestyle and beauty tips. She shoots trending videos on TikTok. She talks about anything and everything with her Twitter followers. The model, known for raising her voice especially in political and social issues, is among the most influential celebrities in social media. Her funny videos she shoots with his family and her sarcastic posts are highly loved by social media users. She also uses her platform to raise brand awareness on her cookbooks.

Karlie Kloss

Famous Victoria’s Secret  model Karlie Kloss has a YouTube channel that is very interesting to those who are interested in the fashion industry. Karlie Kloss gives backstages from the runways, her collaborations, and shows her followers what’s behind the scenes of modeling. She also shoots many videos with social media influencers from different platforms.

Romee Strijd

Yet another Victoria’s Secret star Romee Strijd can be counted as YouTube star now. For a few years, she has been posting every part of her life weight struggles, difficulties of being a mother, magazine photoshoots, her diets and more. Even though she is a highly acclaimed model, Romee is not afraid of speaking about taboo topics on her platform.

Jessica Alba


Actress Jessica Alba is also very active on Instagram. She promotes her own beauty brand “Honest Beauty” on her account while sharing her own experiences and doing interviews with field professionals.
