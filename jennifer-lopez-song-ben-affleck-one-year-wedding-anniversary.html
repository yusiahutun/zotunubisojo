Jennifer Lopez Writes Romantic Song for Ben Affleck for Their Wedding Anniversary

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

One year after the two officially became husband and wife, Jennifer Lopez is commemorating her secret Las Vegas wedding with Ben Affleck with a song.

In her fan newsletter, On the JLo, the multi-hyphenate shared lyrics for a song titled “Midnight Trip to Vegas.” The song detailed the stress that comes with planning a big Hollywood wedding (“It’s crowded with families and agents / Room reservations / Which destination / Paps’ helicopters / Event of the ages”). Lopez and Affleck’s solution? Taking their blended family on a midnight road trip to Las Vegas, where they could get married without the rest of the world knowing (“Didn’t tell a soul ’bout how it’s goin’ down tonight”).

The lyrics reflect the couple’s real Vegas wedding, which took place in July 2022. For the occasion, Lopez’s kids, twins Emme and Max Muñiz, joined the couple for their nuptials.

The “Let’s Get Loud” singer previously revealed that her and Affleck’s first experience wedding planning 20 years ago inspired their decision to get hitched in Vegas. “This time, I absolutely had a little PTSD. I was like, Is this happening? Is this really happening? And we were so happy, and of course, it was happening, but I just felt the wedding was so stressful,” she said on Jimmy Kimmel Live! “Ben just says, ‘F**k it, let's just go to Vegas and get married tonight.’”

Lopez teased the song in her latest Instagram post, in which she shared a few selfies of herself in the bejeweled Valentino minidress she wore during her anniversary date with Affleck on Sunday.

“One year since our Midnight Trip To Vegas … 🤍♠️❤️ Go to On The JLo for a special surprise… #ThisIsMeNow,” she wrote in the caption.
