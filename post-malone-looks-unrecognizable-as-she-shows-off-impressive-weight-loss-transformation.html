Post Malone looks 'unrecognizable' as he shows off impressive weight loss transformation

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 9, 2024

Just weeks after revealing how he achieved his impressive 55-pound weight loss, Post Malone has taken to Instagram to show off his accomplishment.

The music star, whose real name is Austin Richard Post, recently revealed that he was able to lose over 50 pounds just by cutting out his soda intake. Now, he’s taken to the ‘gram to show off the results.

Post Malone looks ‘unrecognizable’ as he shows off weight loss

Taking to his 24 million Instagram followers, the Sunflower singer posted a mirror selfie of himself looking dapper in all black.

While holding one hand in his pocket, the Rockstar singer sported leather from head to toe, with a blazer, pants, and boots.

He then mixed it up a bit, accessorizing it with a leopard print belt and baseball cap, as he captioned the post: “Introducing Viceroy Chungus VonBattlepass, I love you.”

Fans ask if the star is single

Upon seeing the post on their feeds, fans rushed to the comments to compliment the star on how good he looks, even making jokes in the comments.

“I need to quit drinking soda,” joked one.

“Is he single? asking for me,” penned another.

Another laughed: “Telling my children this is their real father.”

“Who is this??” questioned another.

One fan complimented: “That’s what I call a hard flex broski”

Unfortunately, Posty isn’t single, he’s actually engaged, although he keeps the identity of his fiance private.

Post Malone made ‘huge lifestyle changes’ for his daughter

Opening up about his lifestyle changes on the Call Her Daddy podcast earlier this month, the 28-year-old revealed that becoming a father made him rethink some of his habits, including ‘slowing down on drinking’.

“I guess not being able to be there for my baby, which is a new fear,” he explained. “That’s why I tried to slow down on drinking, to take better care of my body.”

Malone and his fiance, welcomed their first child, a daughter in May 2022.
