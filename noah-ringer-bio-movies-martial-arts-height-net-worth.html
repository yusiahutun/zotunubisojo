Noah Ringer Bio, Movies, Martial Arts, Height, Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

Credited for movies like The Last Airbender for playing Aang and Emmett in Cowboys & Aliens, Noah Andrew Ringer is a talented American actor. The 23-year-old actor and martial arts practitioner Ringer is one of the leading young actors in the industry. He started his career in 2010 appearing in The Last Airbender.

As per reports, Noah is currently single and focused on his career. Ringer is a first-degree black belt in Taekwondo. To know more about Noah Ringer’s net worth, movies, martial art life, scroll below.

Who Is Noah Ringer? His Bio And Height

Noah Andrew Ringer was born on 18th November 1996, in Dallas, Texas, USA. As a child, Noah was very introvert and was homeschooled by his parents. There is not much information about his parents.

Noah’s mother enrolled him in a martial academy, ATA Martial Arts in Carrollton, Texas. Noah is a native American and in his childhood, he was nicknamed Avatar by his friends. Ringer was born under the sun sign Scorpio and has a height of 5 feet 5 inch.

Noah Ringer’s Martial Arts

Ringer fell in love with martial arts and improved very quickly. In December 2008, Noah received his first-degree black belt and started to compete in tournaments. Noa’s first tournament came in the World Championship in Arkansas.

Ringer won every category in the tournament where he was eligible to participate. He started training in Extreme Martial Arts and became very good at it developing his own moves. Noah uses Eastern Weapons BO Staff very efficiently. In 25 tournaments, Ringer won 100 medals, competitor of the year award, and Texas State Champion title.

Noah also learned Baguazhang, Tai Chi, and Wushu for his movie and in October 2010, Ringer earned Second-degree black belt. Currently, Noah teaches other students at ATA and recently started to practice Choy Li Fut Kung Fu.

Noah Ringer’s Career, Movies

So far, Ringer has appeared in only three films. His first movie is The Last Airbender (2010) where he played the character of Aang. Ringer prepared for one month for the role of Aang learning new forms of martial arts in the process.

Followed by The Last Airbender, he acted in Cowboys & Aliens as Emmett Taggart. The movie is directed by Jon Favreau and is a sci-fi western genre movie. Ringer is handled by Creative Artists Agency and is the winner of several awards.

In March 2011, Noah received nominations for Best Young Artist Award and won the 3rd annual TheSkyKid.com Best Actor Movie award. He bagged the award for his performance in The Last Airbender in May 2011. He is currently shooting for the movie The Peppercorn Chronicles. 

Noah Ringer’s Personal Life

As per reports, Noah is currently single and not dating anyone. Noah is very focused on his career and works very hard. When he is not shooting for the movie, Ringer is busy with his martial arts training and also teaches students at ATA.

Besides acting, Noah attends many local events and charities contributing to the community. Noah is active on Facebook mostly than on Instagram. Ringer loves to hang out with his friends, watch movies, reading, editing, and creating videos.

His friends nicknamed him Avatar from the character of the animated television series Avatar: The Last Airbender.
