Ashley Graham's best casual looks How to dress like Ashley Graham

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, June 15, 2024

1

Invest in a waist belt

Getty

If you're lucky enough to have a figure like Ashley Graham's, milk corsets for all they're worth. They provide a great way to nip in your waist, even if you're wearing a basic maxi dress or a simple T-shirt.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

2

Make a trench-coat your best friend

Getty

In the same way as a wrap dress, a trench-coat is one of the most flattering pieces in anyone's wardrobe. Take your cue from Graham and wear it with heels and statement earrings for an elegant take on the look.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

3

Employ one-tone dressing

Getty

A trick used by everyone from Melania Trump to Bella Hadid, dressing head-to-toe in one colour is an easy way to lengthen your frame and ensure your outfit looks more expensive than it actually is.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

4

Wear your LBDs everywhere

Getty

Graham is rarely spotted dressed-down. Want to tap into her confidence? Go for glamour all the way and wear your little black dresses wherever you are.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

5

Make the most of your assets

Getty

Don't feel you have to cover up your cleavage just because you were blessed in that department. Take a tip from Graham and embrace low-cut necklines, especially when they're in the form of lady-like, silky frocks.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

6

Opt for cropped

Getty

Wearing shorter-length jackets and coats will help make your legs look longer than ever. Exaggerate this even further by adding heels.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

Bare your midriff

Getty

Not everyone feels brave enough to wear a crop top, but just let a slither of skin show through to avoid feeling like you are revealing too much.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

8

Go down a band size with your strapless bras

Getty

Graham gave this genius piece of advice on her Instagram account: "Tip for wearing a strapless bra – go down a size only in the band. Keeps the girls lifted without suffocating them."

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

9

Team your skinnies with flattering tops

Getty

If you don't feel like wearing tight clothing from top to bottom, team your skinny jeans with flattering wrap tops that tie in at the waist but don't hug your whole torso.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

Remember sheer dressing is not just for teenagers...

Getty

...or size-six models. Don't be afraid to embrace the trend, but we'd recommend making sure you pair it with something more demure if you're looking for a grown-up spin, as Graham has done here with her ankle-length skirt.
