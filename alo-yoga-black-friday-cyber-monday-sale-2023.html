Best Alo Yoga Cyber Monday Sale 2023 Deals

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, February 9, 2024

When you think of celebrity style, your mind might drift toward the runways at Paris Fashion Week and Clueless-type closets full of couture designers. But really, few brands are as intertwined with A-listers as Alo Yoga. The athleticwear and athleisure label is regularly spotted on all the Fashion Week and red carpet regulars—Hailey, Kendall, Lori, Gigi, and so on—when they’re out to brunch or headed to a workout. A chance to coordinate with these modern style icons finally arrived with Alo Yoga’s Cyber Monday sale for 2023.

Here’s the fine print: Alo Yoga’s Cyber Monday sale began back on November 20, and lasts until Tuesday, November 28. As far as activewear goes, it’s one of the best Cyber Monday fashion deals you’ll find throughout the entirety of the sale holiday. Alo’s whole store is on sale for 30 percent off, and some previous markdowns are up to 70 percent off.

The lineup of sale items covers everything from the best matching workout sets to yoga mats and hair scrunchies—many that have been spotted on Hollywood’s reigning street style queens. Their most favored pieces, like Alo’s cult-favorite Airlift legging, come backed by hundreds of shopper recommendations praising their buttery-soft feel and durability. Lest you assumed Alo Yoga’s sale was just about stretch and Spandex, the brand is also a stealthy resource for excellent outerwear in the fall. This year’s Cyber Monday deals once again include its top-rated Gold Rush puffer jacket in time for the holidays.

In other words, Alo Yoga’s current sale will restock your gym bag—and potentially your entire casual wardrobe—in the style of the best-dressed celebrities. And if there’s a celebrity style fanatic on your gifting list this holiday, I’ll note that even Alo’s coveted Advent Calendar is included in the sale.

Below, I’ve gathered the top items available in Alo Yoga's Cyber Monday sale right now. They include a mix of light fall coats, supportive sports bras, and top-rated leggings to take you through your next workout. With the sale coming to an end very soon, now's the moment to re-up your activewear selection with the picks ahead.
