The Black Keys singer Dan Auerbach is expecting a baby with new fiancee Jen Goodall

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, February 28, 2024

The Black Keys singer and guitarist Dan Auerbach is expecting a child.

The 35-year-old has just become engaged to Jen Goodall, who debuted her baby bump at the Grammys on Sunday.

A rep for the happy couple confirmed the good news with Us Weekly following the awards show, where Auerbach and his bandmate Patrick Carney were nominated for three Grammys.

The Black Keys singer and guitarist Dan Auerbach is expecting a child with fiancee Jen Goodall, pictured on Sunday night at the Grammys in LA

Auerbach has a six-year-old daughter with his ex wife Stephanie Gonis.

Gonis and the rocker finalized their divorce in August 2013. They split was highly publicized due to certain Rock 'n' Roll details - including a war over a lock of Bob Dylan's hair. 

Meanwhile The Black Keys were almost delayed from going on tour earlier this year when Carney suffered a shoulder injury in the Caribbean.

Happy: The duo looked ecstatic over their good news

The 34-year-old Grammy winner was enjoying the tropical waters of St Barts when a large wave slammed him down to the ocean floor.

According to TMZ, Patrick was then rushed to the hospital for treatment to reset a dislocated shoulder.

Patrick had been enjoying the French Caribbean island as a break between legs of the Black Keys' Turn Blue World Tour.

Touring men: The rocker is half of the team behind The Black Keys, pictured in December in Orlando Florida

He and his Black Keys partner Auerbach, were scheduled to resume that tour in February, which they thankfully made good on. 

If they had missed the tour, it would have been the first major setback for the rock duo since their breakout album, Brothers, was released in 2010.

They won three Grammy Awards for that release, followed by three more for the Black Keys' next album, El Camino.

The group's current album, 2014's Turn Blue, has been named a number-one record in both the United States and Australia.
