See 'Last Man Standing' Star Kaitlyn Dever Shut Down Red Carpet in a Sheer Corset Dress

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, March 15, 2024

When it comes to the Oscars, stars step out in their best looks...or at least attempt to. The same goes for the Vanity Fair after party, but if you ask us, the looks at the star-studded event deserve awards of their own. Case in point? Actress Kaitlyn Dever's stunning outfit.

Although not nominated for any Academy Awards, the former Last Man Standing and Justified actress stepped out on the red carpet to once again show everyone that when it comes to major fashion moments, she's the one to watch.

Dressed in a custom Dolce & Gabbana gown, the 26-year-old looked stunning in her dress designed with a strapless corset top and sheer bottom. As if we weren't already floored with the classy look, according to the designer's Instagram page, the entire dress was embroidered with Swarovski crystals and glass beads with the boning detail.

The actress shared her red carpet snapshot on Instagram, and needless to say, fans jumped at the first opportunity to share their opinions. Luckily, there was no need to phone the fashion police because everyone was in agreement that Kaitlyn looked absolutely jaw-dropping.
