Tom Waes Biography, Age, Height, Wife, Net Worth, Wiki

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, February 11, 2024

Tom Waes is a talented and famous Belgian actor, director, television presenter. His work is recognized by his audience and they love to watch him on screen. He is famous for his programs like Het Sex De Pauw, Tomtesterom, Wauters vs Waes and Tragger Hippy. He is also the ambassador of the non-profit organization Sea Shepherd whose mission is to protect marine life worldwide. Tom was born on 7th November, 1968 in Merksem, Belgium.

His original name is Tom Yvo Adrien Waes and likes to keep his personal life private and does not like sharing it on social media. Tom studied at a local high school and later went on to study Germanic philology for a year. He pursued a driving course in Scotland and then worked as a deep sea driver on oil rigs for seven years.

Tom Waes Career

Tom started his career by making films for De Quizmaster and The Last Show. He rose to fame because of his role as the brother of Bart De Pauw in Het Gender De Pauw in the year 2005. In partnership with Koen Van Impe and Jan Van Looveren he made the comedy TV program Tragger Hippy which was a super hit. Later in 2007 he was selected to be on the jury for the first series of the comedy Casino Cup.
