Emily Ratajkowski's Sparkly Bronze Party Dress Is Her Best One Yet

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, February 19, 2024

There is not a sheer minidress that Emily Ratajkowski will not rock, but her latest party dress may be her best one yet.

The model and My Body author stepped out this Monday night in a glittery bronze mini that perfectly embodies all our fall party dressing dreams. The glamorous see-though piece featured a mock neck, long sleeves, and an asymmetrical train that wrapped around the skirt and fell nearly to her ankles.

Ratajkowski took a style cue from fellow models Kendall Jenner’s and Hailey Bieber’s fall wardrobes and expertly styled the minidress with sheer black tights from Calzedonia and strappy black sandals with stiletto heels. She accessorized simply with a glossy black mini shoulder bag and seemed to wear no jewelry.

For her beauty look, the High Low With EmRata podcast host stuck to her usual straight hairdo, bronzy makeup, and glossy lip.

TheImageDirect.com

Ratajkowski wore the glittering party look to attend Odell Beckham Jr.’s birthday bash following the 2023 CFDA Fashion Awards, held in New York City on Monday night. Other celebrity guests included Lori Harvey, Ashley Graham, Winnie Harlow, and pro sports stars James Harden, Saquon Barkley, Stefon Diggs, and Sauce Gardner.

At the CFDA gala, Ratajkowski walked the red carpet in a dreamy, airy, light-green minidress from Tory Burch’s Spring/Summer 2024 Ready-to-Wear collection, matching silky green heels, and a diamond tennis bracelet by Brilliant Earth. She wore bronze makeup, golden eye shadow, and a baby-pink manicure; she had her hair styled in a soft, wavy do.
