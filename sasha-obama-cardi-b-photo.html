Sasha Obama Was Photographed Hanging Out with Cardi B and Offset

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, March 26, 2024

Sasha Obama has kept under the radar since the former first family moved out of the White House; so what has the cool 16-year-old been up to? Aside from celebrating her sweet 16, joining in on her family's envious vacations, and snagging the best room in their new post-presidency home, her list of Cool Girlâ„¢ activities includes casual backstage hangouts with Cardi B and Offset.

A photo from this weekend's Broccoli City Festival in Washington D.C. recently surfaced, showing the former first daughter hanging out with hip-hop's favorite new couple backstage. In the shot, the teen wears a white crop top, distressed jeans, layered necklaces, and white sunglasses with the smiling pair.

Naturally, Twitter freaked out over the encounter.

We already knew Sasha was a music fan, after she made previously appearances at Lollapalooza music festival in Chicago and was seen rapping along to Chance the Rapper at the 2016 White House Christmas tree lighting. Her latest sighting only further confirms her fandom.

Plus, Sasha didn't just see Cardi B at any performance; she saw Cardi B at her last performance before giving birth to her first child. Last week, the Bronx-bred rapper announced that her Broccoli Fest gig would be her last one "for a little while" due to her pregnancy. (She'll return to the stage in time for her opening slot on Bruno Mars' tour.)

Sasha is one of the lucky fans to to see the hip-hop star pre-delivery; and Cardi and Offset's baby has already had the privilege of meeting an Obama before birth.
