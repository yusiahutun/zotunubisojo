Lucas Congdon Bio, Mother, Age, Wife, House &amp; Net Worth

An American TV actor Lucas Congdon is famous for his television show Insane Pools: Off the Deep End. The show is broadcasted on Animal Planet. Its first episode was broadcasted on 20th February 2015. Besides his television career, Congdon is also a backyard pool and exterior designer.

Congdon is living a blissful life with his spouse Galen Congdon. His wife is a beauty blogger. From their relationship, the husband and wife share two kids.

About Congdon

Lucas Congdon was born in Vermont, United States. He was raised in the mountain of Vermont by his mother Jane Werley. His nationality is American and belongs to the white ethnicity. However, the details of his father are still unavailable. As of 2021, his age is around 40.

Congdon attended Montana State University in 1996. He graduated with a degree in Landscape Designing in 1997. At the age of 14, Congdon began to cut and craft stones.

His Wife Galen Congdon

The exterior designer Lucas Congdon is in a marital relationship with his life partner, Galen Congdon, a beauty blogger. Furthermore, she is also a Vice President of Lucas Lagoons, Inc. However, the details of their wedding such as date and place are yet to unveil.

They are one of the loving and caring parents as well as the perfect partners. Both the couple supports each other. Moreover, they also spend quality time together during their free time visiting new places along with their kids. Congdon often shares pictures of his family on his Instagram account.

Career Highlights

At the age of 14, Lucas Congdon began to cut and craft stones. Later in 2001, he moved to Florida and settled down. Moreover, he also became a member of FSPA & APSP. In 2003, Congdon began his own business Lucas Lagoons. His company creates grand, nature-inspired backyard oases across the country.
