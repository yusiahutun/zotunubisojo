When is the Championship play-off final? Huddersfield vs Reading tickets, TV channel, kick-off time

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 6, 2024

THE richest game in football is drawing ever closer as the Championship play-off finalists look to join Newcastle and Brighton in the top flight next season.

Last year Hull City were victorious at Wembley as a Mo Diame thunderbolt sunk Sheffield Wednesday.

Here is everything you need to know ahead of this season's £190m showpiece.

When is the Championship play-off final?

The Championship play-off final between Huddersfield and Reading will be played on Monday, May 29.

Kick-off will be at 3pm at Wembley.

The Bank Holiday fixture will be the last of a packed Wembley schedule that weekend, with the FA Cup final and League 2 play-off final being played on the Saturday and Sunday respectively.

What TV channel is it on?

Football League fixtures are televised exclusively on Sky Sports.

The match is likely to be shown on Sky Sports 1 and 1HD.

Coverage tends to begin and hour before the big event so the programme will probably start at 2pm.

Can I get tickets?

Reading tickets are being sold via See Tickets, while Huddersfield are selling their allocation via their ticket office.

Both clubs have announced that the remainder of their allocation is on general sale.

Adult tickets cost between £36 and £98, which is a £2 reduction from last season in the price of the cheapest ticket.

Club Wembley packages are available for £349 and supporters can register their interest by contacting the FA.
