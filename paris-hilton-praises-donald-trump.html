Paris Hilton praises Donald Trump

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, May 27, 2024

Getty Images

Just when we thought her triumphant comeback was almost complete, Paris Hilton has thrown a spanner in the works by showing her appreciation for Donald Trump.

Her praise is more unfortunately timed than normal - this week the US President defended alt-right protesters, claiming that those who took part in the neo-Nazi rally last weekend were "fine people".

Hilton has known Trump since she was 12. He has previously said that when he met her for the first time when she was pre-pubescent, he asked "Who the hell is that?" The politician also admitted to having watched her sex tape.

And yet, Hilton - now a successful businesswoman in her own right - still thinks he's a good man.

"I've known him since I was a little girl. And he's always been so nice, so respectful and sweet," she told Marie Claire.

She refuses to believe the multiple women who have accused Trump of sexual assault.

"I think that they are just trying to get attention and get fame," she said.

His infamous "grab them by the pussy" line doesn't concern her either. Like Trump himself, she writes it off as male bravado.

"I've heard guys say the craziest things ever, because I'm always around guys, and I listen to them speak," she says.
