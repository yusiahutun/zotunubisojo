How did Jim Rash start his Professional Career?

As Mr. Grayson/Stitches in the 2005 movie Sky High, as Fenton on That ’70s Show, and as Andrew the “whore houseman” on Reno 911, Jim became well-known for his performances. He appeared in the final Friends episode and played Head T.A. Philip in Slackers. For four seasons, Rash portrayed the Marquess of Queensbury in the animated series Mike Tyson Mysteries on Adult Swim.

He and his comedic partner Nat Faxon tried their hand at writing a script in 2005 for a program called Adopted, but it was never picked up.

They later wrote the novel with the same name that appeared on the Black List in 2008 as the basis for the 2011 film The Descendants. The film’s later director Jim Taylor and screenwriter and director Alexander Payne substantially revised the script.

Rash played Craig Pelton on Community from 2009 to 2015. He gained a huge following thanks to his performance as Craig Pelton. During the fourth season of the show, he also contributed to the scripts.

Since the 2017 DuckTales reboot, he has provided the original voice for Gyro Gearloose, a character from the Donald Duck universe.
