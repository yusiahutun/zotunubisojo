Alex Sensation is a popular, award-winning radio DJ, multicultural musical tastemaker, and recording artist.

DJ Alex Sensation - Married to Wife Diana Two Sons

The popular DJ Alex Sensation is happily married to his wife Diana with two sons. Sensation and his wife, however, have been together for more than thirty years. In 2010, Alex announced on Twitter that he had been together with his partner for 16 years.

DJ Alex Sensation and his newlywed wife Diana at their wedding. SOURCE: Instagram (@alexsensation)

"Here's my beautiful wife we are celebrating 16 years together and we wanted to share this pic with u guys," wrote Alex on Twitter. Not to mention, according to Sensation, it was love at first sight for him. And, in the late 2010s, Alex and Diana finally exchanged their marital vows.

Sensation and his bride had a Christian wedding in a church. The musician donned a black tuxedo with a black bow tie while his bride donned a white bridal fishtail gown.

DJ Alex Sensation with his wife Diana and their son. SOURCE: Instagram (@alexsensation)

Alex and his spouse are also parents to two children. The identity of Sensation's sons is a mystery other than the fact that their father shares their pictures on Instagram sometimes. Alex and his partner work together to raise their kids and the lovely family of four resides in New York.

Career & Net Worth of Alex Sensation

Alex Sensation began his DJing career at sixteen by playing records from his collection at gigs in nightclubs. In 1999 he was named one of the top ten Hispanic music DJs in New York City.

Check out the clip of Alex Sensation talking about his first album!

Likewise, in 2009 he released the mix album Alex Sensation & Friends: La Mega Mezcla on Planet Records, and in 2013 he received an ACE (Association of Latin Entertainment Critics) Award. Determined to be successful, his passion for music led him to achieve higher success and he has thousands of fans with him.

Though many of his fans know him from his music, very few know him as a philanthropist. He has been selflessly involved with many charities and donating to the helpless. After all, the compassionate DJ is expected to hold a net worth of more than $1 million.

Quick Facts of DJ Alex Sensation

  • Alex was born and brought up in Colombia before he moved to the States.
  • Sensation worked four different jobs to support his family before his music career kicked off.
  • DJ Alex owns two poodles.
  • At 17, Alex worked for the popular club Copacabana.
  • Sensation has been involved with Latino FM Mega La 97.9 for years.
